# Post Earnings Drift

Post-earnings drift is a tactical options strategy that capitalizes on directional price movement occurring in the days and weeks following a company's earnings announcement, distinct from the immediate gap reaction. Traders identify stocks that have beaten earnings expectations and establish positions to ride the subsequent drift, with entry timing and position sizing informed by research rather than reactive pullback-waiting after large gap-ups. The strategy requires disciplined trade management and exit planning to convert the identified drift opportunity into realized profit, rather than allowing positions to drift without a defined exit framework.
