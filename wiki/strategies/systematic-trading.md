# Systematic Trading

Systematic trading in options involves applying disciplined, rule-based decision-making processes rather than relying on emotion or intuition, with success depending on identifying a genuine edge and maintaining strict position sizing. The approach emphasizes iterative skill development and hypothesis-driven research, which can be enhanced through automation tools and data infrastructure that generate signals and systematize strategy development. Rather than complex multi-leg strategies, systematic traders focus on consistent execution of well-defined processes across positions like covered strangles and speculative plays, allowing them to adapt to different market regimes while maintaining accountability through performance tracking.
