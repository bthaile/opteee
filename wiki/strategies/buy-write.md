# Buy Write

A buy-write strategy involves buying stock while simultaneously selling a call option against that position, effectively capping upside in exchange for the premium collected from the short call. The strategy is often marketed as a way to generate "income," but the premium received is actually compensation for a real liability—short volatility exposure and foregone upside—rather than true income. The trade is only justified when the sold call is demonstrably overpriced relative to its expected value, not as a blanket income-generation rule, and should be evaluated against the counterfactual of simply selling an equivalent percentage of shares outright.
