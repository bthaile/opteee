# Buying The Panic

Buying the Panic is a contrarian strategy that treats extreme bearish sentiment, forced liquidation, and sharp market sell-offs as entry opportunities rather than signals to exit. The approach relies on identifying dislocations driven by panic rather than fundamental deterioration—such as tariff-induced volatility or event-driven crashes—and using options to establish positions at depressed prices and elevated implied volatility. Practitioners frame these moments as temporary extremes that resolve toward recovery, positioning long calls or other bullish structures to capture the subsequent rebound while volatility normalizes.
