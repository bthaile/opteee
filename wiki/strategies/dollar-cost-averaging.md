# Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging in options trading is a systematic approach to adding contracts to existing positions over time while managing total capital deployment, allowing traders to reduce average cost basis incrementally across multiple entries. Rather than deploying all capital at once, the strategy accepts incremental risk on each addition while preserving remaining capital for subsequent opportunities, enabling more efficient allocation across a series of trades. This technique is particularly useful for managing positions that move against the trader, as it provides an active alternative to passive assignment or static equity holding.
