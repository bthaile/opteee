# Overnight Risk Premium

Overnight risk premium refers to the additional return or risk exposure associated with holding positions through overnight and weekend periods when markets are closed, making it a consideration for portfolio hedging and risk management. The strategy involves using leveraged instruments like futures contracts to manage or capitalize on price movements that occur during these gaps in trading, particularly for protecting core positions or gaining direct exposure to overnight market events. This approach is presented as a practical portfolio add-on rather than a primary trading vehicle, useful for traders seeking to hedge existing positions or gain access to moves that occur outside regular trading hours.
