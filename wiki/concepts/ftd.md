# Ftd

FTD (failure to deliver) refers to a situation where a seller fails to deliver securities by the settlement deadline, and in the context of GameStop analysis, FTDs are examined as part of broader settlement mechanics and market structure theories that may influence price movements through delayed delivery cycles and regulatory exemptions. The channel discusses FTDs alongside ETF creation-redemption cycles and total return swaps as potential mechanisms through which short exposure can be masked or delayed, with particular focus on whether systemic failures in market infrastructure contribute to observable trading patterns. The analysis emphasizes the need for rigorous verification of FTD-based theories rather than pattern-seeking, and explores how FTD cycles may interact with options expiration dates and dealer hedging to create predictable price dynamics.
