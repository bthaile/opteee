# Dark Pools

Dark pools are off-exchange trading venues where large block trades are executed away from public order books, representing a significant portion of market structure that can obscure price discovery and retail trader visibility. In options trading, dark pool activity is relevant to understanding how institutional positioning and large trades may influence underlying stock price action and options flow, particularly when analyzing volume spikes or unusual options activity that lacks corresponding visible exchange volume. The channel discusses dark pools alongside related market structure issues like payment for order flow and internalization, emphasizing how these mechanisms can complicate retail traders' ability to interpret options flow and technical signals accurately.
