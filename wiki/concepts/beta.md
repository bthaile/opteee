# Beta

Beta is a measure of an asset's price movement relative to the broader market, used in options trading to construct portfolios with predictable risk characteristics and return profiles. Traders employ beta-weighted delta analysis to align their overall portfolio exposure with market conditions and return targets, ensuring that position sizing and strategy selection reflect both individual trade risk and cumulative portfolio risk. By understanding and managing beta-weighted deltas across multiple positions, traders can systematically build diversified portfolios that achieve specific return goals while staying within defined volatility and drawdown tolerances.
