# Consistency

Consistency in options trading refers to the disciplined, repeated execution of a well-defined trading process and risk-management framework rather than chasing individual trade outcomes or relying on motivation. The concept emphasizes that sustainable long-term returns come from compounding small, moderately-executed actions over time through habit formation and deliberate practice tied to existing routines, combined with realistic position-sizing and aggressive capital accumulation. Treating trading as a business with proper runway, written plans, and process-over-outcome mentality—rather than seeking quick wealth or allowing ego to override discipline—is presented as the foundation for both skill development and genuine financial independence.
