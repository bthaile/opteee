# Price Discovery

Price discovery is the market mechanism by which the true value of an asset is established through the interaction of supply and demand across trading venues. In options trading, price discovery can be distorted by structural market issues such as payment for order flow, dark pool internalization, and off-exchange routing, which fragment liquidity and widen spreads rather than allowing prices to reflect genuine market consensus. Healthy price discovery is characterized by transparent, continuous price formation with broad participation, whereas compromised price discovery—as discussed in the context of GameStop and broader equity markets—results in mispriced options chains and difficulty establishing fair strike values across expirations.
