# Self Awareness

Self-awareness in options trading refers to a trader's honest understanding of their natural risk tolerance, psychological biases, behavioral weaknesses, and execution patterns—knowledge that is foundational to consistent profitability. Rather than relying on technical skill or strategy alone, traders must identify their inherent propensities (such as moving too fast or outcome-focused thinking) and build systematic checks and processes to counteract them. This self-knowledge is developed through detailed trading logs, performance reviews, and deeper psychological work to uncover identity-based beliefs and unconscious self-sabotage that prevent consistent execution. Without brutal honesty about one's actual strengths and limitations, traders are prone to ego-driven blame-shifting and repeated mistakes that undermine long-term success.
