# Andrew Mack

Andrew Mack is an options trader who approaches the market through systematic edge discovery and rigorous testing methodology, drawing parallels between options trading and sports betting as both disciplines centered on identifying positive-expectancy opportunities. He emphasizes observation-driven research into market mechanics and behavioral biases, advocating for deep understanding of volatility dynamics and market regimes before capital deployment. His framework prioritizes payoff ratios over win rates, walk-forward validation of strategies, and psychological discipline to allow tested approaches adequate runway before evaluation, while noting evolving market conditions such as the decline of traditional MOC effects in retail options trading.
