# If You Miss These Steps You're Doing It WRONG #optionstrading

## Summary

Portfolio management in options trading requires a systematic, repeatable process rather than ad-hoc decision-making. The core discipline involves consistently reviewing positions, identifying adjustment opportunities, and right-sizing capital allocation to ensure your portfolio moves you toward your stated objectives.

## Key takeaways

- **Build a reliable portfolio review process** [00:00] — don't turn on the platform and trade randomly; establish a method to assess positions, identify adjustments, and spot new opportunities.
- **Balance capital deployment** [00:22] — ensure you're using enough capital to move the needle toward your goals while avoiding over-leverage or under-deployment.
- **Align portfolio actions with objectives** [00:22] — every position and sizing decision should serve your stated trading objectives, not drift into unrelated risk-taking.
