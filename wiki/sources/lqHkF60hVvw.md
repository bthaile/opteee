# The Reality of Trading Success

## Summary

This video addresses the gap between retail traders' expectations and the actual mechanics of profitable trading. Rather than seeking outsized "get rich quick" edges, sustainable trading success comes from identifying small, explainable market inefficiencies—such as ETF rebalancing effects, inclusion effects, or earnings-related patterns—that compound into solid returns over time. The core message: accepting modest, repeatable edges is the path to real wealth building.

## Key takeaways

- **Robust edges are often unglamorous** [00:00] — ETF rebalancing, inclusion effects, and earnings-driven patterns are deeply rooted in market mechanics but deliver only a few points above market return, not overnight fortunes.
- **Psychological acceptance is the critical step** [00:29] — traders must move past the "dream phase" of trading and embrace the reality that consistent, modest gains compound into substantial wealth.
- **Grindy beats flashy** — small, repeatable edges that traders can explain and defend are more reliable than lottery-ticket strategies.

## Notable quotes
