# Citadel Fine for YOU

## Summary

Eric contextualizes a regulatory fine imposed on Citadel by translating the dollar amount into an equivalent percentage of a retail trader's $100,000 account, illustrating the relative impact of penalties on different market participants. The segment explores how enforcement actions affect institutional versus retail traders and the importance of understanding proportional consequences.

## Key takeaways

- **Proportional penalty analysis** [00:00–00:23]: A fine levied on Citadel is reframed as a percentage of net worth to understand its true impact on the firm relative to what an equivalent fine would mean for a retail trader with $100,000.
- **Empathy through equivalence** [00:00]: Understanding institutional penalties requires translating them into retail-scale equivalents to grasp the actual burden on the firm.
