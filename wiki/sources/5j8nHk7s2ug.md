# Level up your trading with this AI hack - Project No Code #trading #ai

## Summary

This episode demonstrates how to use Claude AI (no-code) to automate trading research and backtesting without writing code. Eric walks through a concrete example: querying Claude to analyze how frequently the S&P 500 trades within all-time highs over a 20-year window, then iterating on the results to refine trading ideas and constraints.

## Key takeaways

- **Prompt engineering for trading research** [00:17–00:37]: Define your constraints precisely (what "all-time high" means, what "within" means, the lookback window), then ask Claude to run the analysis. The AI generates the logic and results without you writing code.
- **Iterative refinement** [00:00–00:17]: You'll discover which analytical pieces interest you most and iterate on them yourself—Claude handles the computational heavy lifting while you focus on strategy development.
- **20-year S&P 500 study example** [00:37]: A practical use case showing how to query historical frequency data (how often SPX is near all-time highs) as a foundation for position-sizing or entry/exit rules.

## Notable quotes

> "I didn't write any of this. All I did was come into Claude code, and I told it this. I want you to run a study over the last 20 years how often the market is within these different constraints of all-time highs."
