# Wrong Indicator = Dead Strategy #optionstrading #trading

## Summary

This video emphasizes the critical relationship between selecting the correct indicators and the underlying profit mechanism of a trading strategy. Eric walks through his preferred indicators and explains why indicator choice must be driven by the specific profit mechanism being traded, not applied generically across all setups.

## Key takeaways

- **Profit mechanism first, indicators second** [00:00] — The foundational principle is to identify your profit mechanism, then overlay indicators that make sense for that specific mechanism; this prevents using the wrong tools for the wrong strategy.
- **Chart setup reflects multiple profit mechanisms** [00:26] — A multi-mechanism trader needs a comprehensive indicator suite on their chart because they attack different profit mechanisms and cannot flip setups between them.
- **Indicator selection is not one-size-fits-all** [00:00] — The video promises a walkthrough of preferred indicators and their specific use cases, implying that each indicator serves a distinct purpose tied to a particular profit mechanism.
