# Finding trades #options #trading #optionstrading

## Summary

When traders struggle to find good trade setups, the issue is typically not a lack of opportunity but an overly narrow skill set. By developing multiple approaches—directional plays at different aggression levels, drift strategies, and volatility-focused trades—traders can consistently identify actionable opportunities across market conditions.

## Key takeaways

- **Expand your toolkit** [00:00–00:21]: Build multiple trading approaches: aggressive upside plays, moderate upside drift strategies, downside equivalents, and volatility-focused trades. This multiplicity ensures you always have viable opportunities.
- **Inability to find trades signals skill gaps** [00:21–00:42]: If you consistently say "I can't find anything that looks good," treat it as an early warning system that your strategy set is too narrow. The solution is to broaden your skill set, not to wait for better market conditions.
- **Opportunity is always present** [00:00]: The beauty of options is that genuine opportunity exists across all market regimes—the constraint is your ability to recognize and execute it.

## Notable quotes
