# Market Update & GME Options Chain Review

## Summary

A brief market recap showing flat action in large-cap indices (SPY, QQQ) while small caps (IWM) diverge downward with heavy sell-side volume. Eric reviews his SPX box-spread positioning (56000 strikes, 18 DTE, ~6.08% annualized yield) and then dives into GME's options chain, noting collapsed implied volatility across expirations and unusually low IV in the earnings cycle (6 DTE) relative to further-out months—a structural anomaly worth monitoring.

## Key takeaways

- **Market structure divergence [00:55–01:54]:** SPY and QQQ flat; IWM rolling over to 8/6 close levels. Small-cap rotation continues despite index-level stability; 75% sell-side volume in IWM at point-of-control level signals caution.
- **Variance risk premium opportunity [02:38–03:03]:** VRP spiked from 8/9 to 8/12; still trading zero-DTE on SPX; IWM also offers VRP plays.
- **SPX box-spread execution [03:31–04:41]:** 56000 strike, 1000-point wide, filled at 997, 18 DTE. Annualized yield 6.08% (including fees), +0.71% over risk-free rate. Return on invested capital much lower; used to deploy idle capital.
- **Yield curve kinks [04:41–05:03]:** 1M SOFR ~5.37%, 2M ~5.295%. 3Y at 3.843%, 5Y at 3.758%, 7Y at 3.805%—5Y depressed relative to 3Y/7Y.
- **GME volume anemia [05:34–06:01]:** 4.4M total volume vs. 10.4M average; 52% buy / 48% sell. Off-exchange volume 47.64% (30-day avg 52%).
- **Order internalization question [06:01–07:29]:** Off-exchange volume reported but internalization degree unknown; dark-pool ATS hits consolidated tape (delayed); internalized orders do not. Calls for research on GME internalization rates.
- **GME options chain collapse [07:52–08:38]:** <100k call volume, 21k put volume. Open interest 6699 calls vs. 337 puts. Front expiration (6 DTE) dominates; quiet positioning 2 weeks out. Contrast with April/May buildup.
- **Theta bleed on near-money calls [08:38–09:22]:** 50-strike calls: 1869 OI, 1600 traded today, ~10¢ theta decay vs. 64–65¢ total premium. 22.5-strike calls: ~45¢ total, massive theta bleed percentage. Requires quick move to profit.
- **IV anomaly: earnings cycle depressed [09:43–10:15]:** 6 DTE (earnings 8/4 scheduled) shows lower IV than 13 DTE and 20 DTE expirations—unusual and noteworthy structural signal.
- **Time & sales: small blocks, deeper ITM [10:36–11:41]:** Most trades small; bigger blocks deeper in-the-money. 125-strike calls still getting pinged (2¢ trades). Longer-dated positions being built; nothing dramatic.
