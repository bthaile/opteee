# Doing Your Own Research MATTERS #optionstrading

## Summary

Eric emphasizes the critical importance of independent research in options trading and investing. Rather than relying on internet personalities or secondhand analysis, traders should develop their own research process using freely available academic resources like SSRN to evaluate claims and build conviction.

## Key takeaways

- **Use SSRN for independent research** [00:00–00:22]: Access ssrn.com (free, no account required) to search academic papers on topics like stock earnings. Sort results by relevance, download count, or recency to find quality research.
- **Do not trust internet personalities blindly** [00:00]: Take all online commentary with skepticism; there is substantial misinformation in circulation.
- **Build your own conviction** [00:00]: Doing your own research is a foundational discipline for avoiding poor trading decisions based on unverified claims.

## Notable quotes

> "You should not take my word for anything. I am an internet guy. You should take what I say with a massive grain of salt and you should do your own research."
