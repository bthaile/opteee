# GME Expiration Cycle Update

## Summary

Market-wide CPI print sparked a pivot toward rate-cut expectations, lifting Russell 2000 and bonds (TLT) while large caps retreated. GME broke above $25 resistance on light volume with a 50/50 buy/sell split; the stock is drifting upward toward its point of control, suggesting a nascent floor. Options activity remains concentrated in front-month expirations (12, 19, 26 July), with notable buying in 19 July $30 calls and put-side hedging activity; probability-of-touch analysis suggests ~16% chance of reaching $27 or $23 by 12 July expiration, insufficient for a gamma ramp.

## Key takeaways

- **Broad market pivot on CPI:** Light CPI print triggered immediate repricing toward rate cuts; Russell 2000 gapped up significantly, TLT rallied, large caps sold off. This sector rotation will cascade into individual names. [00:24]
- **GME broke $25 resistance:** Stock cracked above $25 on light volume; while some traders cite ALOS resistance, the break is factual. [01:38]
- **Slow drift upward toward point of control:** Since 1 July low, GME has drifted higher; current price action suggests a floor is forming, though it can collapse at any time. [02:22]
- **Volume split flipped to buy-heavy:** Yesterday was 95% sell volume; today is ~50/50 buy/sell (45% buy, 55% sell). This shift despite light overall volume is "telling" of underlying strength. [02:46]
- **Options volume cratered:** Yesterday >300k contracts; today <200k. Activity concentrating in 12, 19, 26 July expirations. [04:25]
- **19 July $30 calls surging:** Over 20,500 open interest in 19 July $30 calls (up from 59k total OI); >20k transactions observed. Multiple mid-price fills suggest institutional or coordinated buying, though inconclusive whether single actor. [05:36]
- **Theta decay brutal on short-dated calls:** 19 July $30 calls priced ~$0.795, losing 11–12 cents daily (~13.8% daily decay). Difficult to profit without large directional move. [06:54]
- **Call/put OI gap tightening:** Call OI 900k vs. put OI 500k (previously 2–4× wider). More hedging activity on put side (34% buying puts vs. 26% selling). [08:44]
- **Put-side OI dispersed across expirations:** Puts scattered across 19 July, 17 Jan 2025, 18 Oct 2024—suggesting long-term hedging. Calls remain concentrated in front months. [09:04]
- **12 July expiration looks quiet:** Medium OI (~16k max strike), light activity. 12 July probability cone: ~16% chance of touching $27 or $23 by expiration. [11:46]
- **26 July building out:** 14,788 volume vs. 11k OI—clear position opening. This is becoming the "more interesting" expiration. [12:26]
- **No gamma ramp catalyst visible:** Light OI across entire cycle; insufficient stacking to trigger meaningful gamma hedging or squeeze. Stock has "puncher's chance" but no structural catalyst yet. [14:02]
