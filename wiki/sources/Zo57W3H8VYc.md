# Looking for High Volatility? Find Stocks with Earnings #optionstrading

## Summary

This video teaches traders how to identify stocks with upcoming earnings announcements as a reliable source of elevated implied volatility. The core insight is that earnings events create predictable volatility spikes, making them a systematic hunting ground for options strategies that benefit from high IV environments—particularly outside of active earnings seasons when IV is suppressed elsewhere.

## Key takeaways

- **Earnings as a volatility source** [00:00] — Upcoming earnings announcements are a consistent, searchable source of elevated implied volatility; check earnings calendars systematically when general market IV is low.
- **Seasonal earnings cycles** [00:00] — Earnings seasons cluster (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4); outside these windows, IV tends to compress, making individual earnings plays stand out as relative opportunities.
- **Timing and opportunity** [00:00] — When broader market volatility is suppressed, earnings-driven IV becomes a high-probability hunting ground for premium-selling or volatility-based strategies.
