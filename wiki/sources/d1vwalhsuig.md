# GME & CHWY Options Expiration Week

## Summary

Market closed with a modest lift in small-cap breadth (IWM +3%) following Jackson Hole signals on inflation, but GME failed to participate meaningfully, closing flat at ~$22.21 despite 287 net new highs across the NYSE. Chewy showed similar sideways consolidation ahead of earnings (Aug 28), with notably higher options activity across multiple expirations and bullish call-to-put ratio (2:1). Both underlyings exhibit low volume and quiet options chains typical of expiration week.

## Key takeaways

- **GME price action disappointment** [00:41–02:45]: Opened $22.25, closed $22.21 (flat); despite IWM rallying 3% on Fed inflation signals, GME failed to participate. Market breadth strong (287 net highs vs. 136 yesterday), but GME volume remained light (~4M shares, below 20-day average). Sideways range-bound since Aug 9 is holding, but sale volume insufficient to push price lower—mixed signal.

- **GME options bleed-off** [04:13–04:39]: 574k calls at start of day; 100k expired. Volatility for this cycle dropped from 80%+ to 72% after host closed volatility trade early. Activity migrating to next expiration cycle (standard behavior). Reminder: earnings scheduled for Sept 4 (pre-market).

- **GME week: minimal large trades** [05:44–06:12]: Only two composite trades >$100k all week—June 25 calls ($364k) and Jan 26 calls ($175k). Larger positioning is far out in time; front expiration remains quiet.

- **Chewy consolidation into earnings** [06:35–07:48]: Trading sideways; earnings Aug 28. Options activity notably busier than GME across multiple expirations. Bullish positioning: 32k calls vs. 15k puts (2:1 ratio). Earnings vol at 128%, drops to 96% post-earnings (typical crush).

- **Chewy options week: subdued aggregate size** [09:18–09:43]: Call-side ask-side activity sparse; smallest aggregate size relative to GME. Options chain quiet; no major positioning detected.

- **Expiration week reminder** [08:09–08:58]: Multiple worthless expirations; host emphasizes smarter options entry strategies to avoid weekly premium bleed. Link to educational video provided in notes.
