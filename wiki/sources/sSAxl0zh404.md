# Agustin Lebron You NEED To Talk With Trusted Traders #trading

## Summary

Agustin Lebron emphasizes that emotional control alone cannot salvage a losing strategy—traders must first establish genuine edge. The core insight is that building a small, trusted peer group (3–5 people) for daily feedback and reality-checking accelerates learning and decision-making far more effectively than large online communities.

## Key takeaways

- **Edge before emotion** [00:00–00:20]: No amount of emotional discipline can turn a losing strategy profitable; edge is the prerequisite.
- **Small trusted circle over large communities** [00:20–00:41]: A group of 3–5 people you talk to most days, where you can stress-test ideas and get honest feedback, is more valuable than large Discord groups or forums.
- **Peer feedback as acceleration** [00:41]: Getting another set of eyes on your thesis—"Does this sound reasonable to you?"—is one of the most powerful learning accelerators for traders.

## Notable quotes

> "There's no amount of emotional control that will turn a losing strategy into a profitable strategy."

> "You need to have a small group of people that you can talk to where you trust their judgment they trust your judgement."
