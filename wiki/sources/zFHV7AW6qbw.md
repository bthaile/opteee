# Do the work

## Summary

A brief motivational segment emphasizing that consistent, disciplined work over years is the foundation of successful options trading. Eric reflects on 16 years of continuous analysis and the reality that freedom in trading comes only through sustained effort, not shortcuts.

## Key takeaways

- **The work never stops** [00:00] — even after 16 years of trading, daily analysis and preparation remain non-negotiable.
- **Outcome requires process** [00:00] — the "really cool" outcome (freedom) is unlocked only by those willing to do the unglamorous daily work.
- **Shortcuts lead to failure** [00:00] — skipping the foundational work in options trading is a guaranteed path to losses.

## Notable quotes

> "everybody loves the idea of making a bunch of money trading options but not too many people put in the requisite work to make it happen"

> "the outcome is really cool you unlock your freedom but if you think that you're going to get there by not doing the work you're just setting yourself up for failure"
