# Momentum Research Tweak & Test #optionstrading

## Summary

This video walks through the iterative research and backtesting process for refining a momentum strategy. Eric demonstrates how to systematically test different lookback periods (1-month, 3-month, 6-month, 12-month), position sizing choices (top 1, 2, 3, or 4 holdings), and filter combinations to identify which parameters generate edge.

## Key takeaways

- **Momentum as a research entry point** [00:00] — Momentum strategies have abundant peer-reviewed research on SSRN, making them ideal for understanding *why* signals work and building intuition around indicator selection.
- **Systematic parameter testing** [00:27] — Test multiple lookback periods (1M, 3M, 6M, 12M) and position counts (top 1 vs. top 2 vs. top 3 vs. top 4) to isolate which combination produces the best risk-adjusted returns.
- **Iterative filter addition** [00:51] — Build complexity incrementally: start with a base momentum signal, then layer in short-side logic, additional filters, and mixed-lookback approaches to refine edge without overfitting.
- **Research-driven hypothesis testing** — Use published academic work to inform initial signal design, then validate or refute each hypothesis through backtesting rather than intuition alone.

## Notable quotes
