# Become A Better Trading By Being A Data Analyst #optionstrading

## Summary

This video frames the practice of trading as a data-analysis discipline. The core thesis is that traders should synthesize research findings, identify trends, and critically deconstruct completed trades by decomposing P&L attribution across the Greeks to verify whether they isolated their intended edge.

## Key takeaways

- **Synthesize and iterate** [00:00] — Combine all research observations into coherent patterns; use this synthesis to identify areas for future iteration.
- **Post-trade deconstruction** [00:28] — After closing a position, decompose P&L by Greek (delta, gamma, theta, vega) to confirm you captured the edge you intended, not accidental exposure.
- **Data-driven feedback loop** — Treat your trading log as a dataset; use it to refine process and edge identification over time.
