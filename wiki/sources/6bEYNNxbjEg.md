# Empowering Your Worth with Lossdog Tom Sosnoff | The Outlier Podcast

## Summary

Tom Sosnoff discusses Lossdog, a platform that provides real-time job market data and salary analytics, drawing parallels to how financial markets price securities. The platform aggregates real-time job data to show users their market value and provides analytics to help them increase their earning potential.

## Key takeaways

- **Real-time job pricing model** [00:00–00:23]: Lossdog functions as a "job quotes" platform analogous to stock quotes, with salary data moving in real time based on market conditions rather than static salary surveys.
- **Resume-to-valuation workflow** [00:00–00:23]: Users upload or manually fill in resume information, and the platform returns industry-average compensation and personalized market value based on live data feeds.
- **Information-driven value creation** [00:23]: The more granular data users provide, the more actionable analytics the platform returns, creating a feedback loop that increases user earning potential.
- **Founder track record** [00:00]: Sosnoff references building and exiting multiple companies for hundreds of millions to billions of dollars, establishing credibility for the venture.
