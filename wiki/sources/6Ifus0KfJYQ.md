# Find Better Trades: Stock Market Analysis Process

## Summary

Eric walks through a three-level framework for analyzing the stock market: starting with macroeconomic indicators (GDP, inflation, unemployment, FOMC), then rotating to broad market indices and breadth metrics, and finally drilling down into individual sectors and stocks. The process uses free tools like FRED and thinkorswim's economic data and charting features to build a disposition that informs position-sizing and trade selection.

## Key takeaways

- **Three-level analysis framework** [00:24–01:04]: Economy → stock market indices → individual stocks. Shorter timeframes require less macro focus, but multi-timeframe traders need all three levels.
- **Economic indicators to monitor** [01:25–02:10]: GDP, CPI, PPI, unemployment, FOMC stance, manufacturing production, and consumer health (housing, credit delinquencies, personal savings). Use FRED dashboards to build custom economic monitoring.
- **thinkorswim economic data tab** [03:40–03:55]: Access monetary data (M1, etc.) and other economic series; many are discontinued, so verify currency. Allows multi-timeframe comparison.
- **Broad market grid setup** [05:15–05:58]: Monitor SPY, QQQ, IWM side-by-side with breadth indicators (% of S&P 500 above 50-MA, net highs/lows for NYSE and NASDAQ), bonds, and VIX. Compare relative performance and proximity to highs.
- **Timeframe-dependent trend reading** [06:42–06:59]: Standard deviation channels may slope differently across 3-month vs. 6-month views; zoom in/out to detect recovery or deterioration. Volume expansion on up/down moves signals conviction.
- **Sector rotation grid** [07:44–08:03]: Use sector ETF grid (XLK, XLB, XLV, etc.) to identify which sectors are outperforming or lagging. Current theme: real estate on a tear, tech slowing relative to other sectors.
- **Earnings and thematic research** [08:22–08:43]: Use ChatGPT or manual research to track earnings beats/misses and identify broader market themes (e.g., real estate vs. tech rotation) that feed into trade idea generation.
- **Individual stock analysis** [09:02]: Technical and fundamental analysis of individual stocks; refer to separate technical-analysis video for detail.
