# It's a SUPERPOWER #investing #trading #stockmarket

## Summary

A brief motivational piece on the role of consistency and habit-formation in achieving trading and investing progress. The core argument: motivation is unreliable, but tying deliberate practice to existing routines creates sustainable edge through compounding small, moderately-executed actions over time.

## Key takeaways

- **Consistency beats motivation** [00:00–00:27]: Motivation is transient and cannot be relied upon for meaningful progress. Consistency—doing things moderately well repeatedly—is the actual superpower.
- **Habit-stacking as a mechanic** [00:00]: Tie new trading/investing practices to activities you already do regularly to reduce friction and increase adherence.
- **Compound effect of moderate execution** [00:27]: You don't need to be spectacular; moderate competence applied consistently over time produces outsized results.

## Notable quotes

> "Consistency is one of the most underappreciated superpowers that exist."

> "I don't do anything spectacular but I do things moderately well super consistently."
