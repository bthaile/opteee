# Can't Trade NO MONEY

## Summary
A foundational lesson on the psychological trap of focusing on profit targets rather than capital preservation. The video argues that obsession with "how much money we're gonna make" leads traders to over-leverage and take excessive risk, ultimately destroying accounts. The core insight: sustainable trading requires disciplined position-sizing and risk-first thinking, not profit-first thinking.

## Key takeaways

- **Profit obsession kills accounts** [00:00] — Traders fixate on potential gains and ignore risk, leading to overleveraged positions and losses.
- **Risk management is the overlooked foundation** [00:00] — Position-sizing and capital preservation must come before profit targets.
- **Process over outcome** [00:00] — Focus on executing a disciplined plan with proper risk controls rather than chasing dollar amounts.

## Notable quotes

> "you can't trade no money… we're too worried about the profits… it leads to taking on too much risk and losing money"
