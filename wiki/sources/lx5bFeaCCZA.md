# BIG GME Call Options Activity and After Hours Move

## Summary

GameStop (GME) experienced significant after-hours price movement and unusual options activity on 2024-07-16, with 344,000 calls traded versus 89,500 puts—a massive call skew concentrated in the front-week 19 July expiration. The 30-strike calls (73,900 OI, 57,600 volume) are approaching in-the-money status, which would trigger dealer gamma hedging and potential buying pressure. Broader call positions are being built across August and October expirations, suggesting institutional positioning ahead of FTD and redemption cycles.

- **After-hours price action**: GME rallied to near $30 after-hours (approximately $29.2x), approaching but not decisively breaking above $30 [00:24]
- **Massive call volume skew**: 344,000 calls vs. 89,500 puts traded; 24,500 of the calls in the front-week 19 July expiration alone [03:34]
