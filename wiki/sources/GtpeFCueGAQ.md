# Your Portfolio Is Finally 250K, Now What? #optionstrading

## Summary

At the 250K account level, the focus shifts from capital preservation to scaling and optimization of proven edge. The core allocation can expand to 100% depending on market conditions, while the speculative allocation begins harvesting volatility risk premium through earnings-focused short premium strategies—naked short straddles, strangles, and single options—sized appropriately to the larger capital base.

## Key takeaways

- **Scaling the proven system** [00:00] — At 250K, you're no longer building; you're optimizing the 25K playbook at larger scale. Core and speculative allocations both flex based on market regime.
- **Dynamic allocation sizing** [00:00–00:24] — Core allocation can reach 100% in favorable conditions; speculative allocation grows with capital. These are guidelines, not fixed rules—they slide based on what the market is doing.
- **Earnings volatility extraction** [00:24–00:45] — The speculative sleeve now explicitly targets earnings plays using naked short premium (short straddles, short strangles, single short options) to capture volatility risk premium inefficiency.
- **Capital-appropriate sizing** [00:45] — Larger capital allows more granular position sizing for premium-selling strategies, enabling better risk management while extracting the same edge.
