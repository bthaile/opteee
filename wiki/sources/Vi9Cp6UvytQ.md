# Tariff impacts to markets

## Summary

Goldman Sachs research suggests Trump tariffs could reduce S&P 500 earnings by 2–3% in the near term. Beyond headline impact, traders should focus on the market vacuums created by tariff uncertainty—gaps that get filled by different market participants, creating tradable opportunities.

## Key takeaways

- **Goldman Sachs tariff scenario:** ~2–3% cut to S&P 500 earnings expected [00:00]
- **Trader's edge:** focus on vacuums created in the market as information flows and different parties step in to fill them [00:26]
- **Process:** always translate macro news into concrete trade opportunities rather than staying at the headline level [00:26]
