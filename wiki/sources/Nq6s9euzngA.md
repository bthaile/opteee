# A Starter $5000 Portfolio #optionstrading

## Summary

This video addresses the practical challenge of trading with limited capital ($5,000), emphasizing that the primary goal should be capital preservation and skill-building rather than aggressive returns. The recommended approach combines short puts in liquid, momentum-driven securities with disciplined position-sizing and a focus on fundamentals to minimize account drawdown while developing trading competence.

## Key takeaways

- **Don't trade a $5K account if possible** [00:00] — but if you must, prioritize not blowing up the account over chasing returns.
- **Focus on fundamentals and good habits** [00:00–00:23] — building a solid foundation is more valuable than early profits.
- **Short puts in momentum names** [00:23] — use liquid, aggressively-moving securities (e.g., SOXL) to generate premium while learning.
- **Favor momentum-style trading** [00:23] — as you build skill, trade securities with directional conviction and volatility to improve edge.
- **Capital preservation is the real edge** — avoid catastrophic losses; let compounding work over time.
