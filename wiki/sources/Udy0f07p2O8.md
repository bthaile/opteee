# Trading Reversals & GME Short Puts Hammered

## Summary

Market pullback across indices with divergent structure: small caps parabolic down (Russell-2000 near retracement), tech more orderly. Breadth deteriorated sharply on Aug 5 but stabilizing. Crowd Strike reversal play discussed as speculative mean-reversion opportunity despite weak macro backdrop. GameStop shows rotation to next expiration cycle with unusual 125 short-put block (~120 contracts, ~$1M premium) likely part of covered-put hedge.

## Key takeaways

- **Indices structure divergence** [01:40–04:02]: Russell-2000 parabolic up, then parabolic down (nearly full retracement); tech pullback more orderly with better support; e-minis in between. Small caps proxy for market weakness due to mixed economic data and rate-cut probability uncertainty.

- **Breadth deterioration then stabilization** [04:24–05:52]: Net highs/lows peaked negative on Aug 5 (331 lows vs 110 highs), now mellowing back toward zero line. SPY point of control still ~512–513; pullback in line with expectations after six-month move but aggressive.

- **Short-volatility opportunity in elevated realized vs implied** [06:31–07:22]: Realized vol significantly above implied creates variance risk premium opportunity. Traded zero-DTE successfully today. Realized-vs-implied spread doesn't automatically signal bad entry; requires positive momentum confirmation (intraday SPY looked "interesting").

- **Economic calendar sensitivity** [07:46–08:51]: PMI came in light (55 vs 56 expected; composite 54.3 vs 55). Market jittery; Aug 7 Consumer Credit and Aug 8 jobless claims will be heavily scrutinized. Any aggressive miss could trigger volatility spike.

- **Crowd Strike reversal setup** [09:11–13:00]: Stock snapped far out of line (rubber-band move); follow-through day present. Typical entry: base below, break above 222 handle with 222 as stop. Speculative; high stop-out probability. Options flow bullish (344k calls vs 328k puts OI; 27% call volume at offer). First resistance ~261, then volume node above. Broader market lacks "wind at back" (tech strong but overall weak), limiting ratio call diagonal appeal; prefer selling puts if playing upside.

- **GameStop rotation to next expiration** [13:29–15:21]: Bounced at 18 handle (prior volume node); lower low concerning, volume anemic. Call OI shifted from front month to Aug 16 (237k calls vs 100k puts in Aug 16 vs 91k/39k in front). Aug 16 OTM calls slightly rich vs puts; pricing in potential jump (appropriate for GME volatility).

- **125 short-put block activity** [16:35–23:07]: ~120 contracts of Aug 16 125 puts sold at bid (9 blocks ~$1M premium). Net close of 6k contracts yesterday-to-today suggests closing volume. Theta collection minimal (~$1/day), inconsistent with pure premium-selling motive. Likely covered-put hedge (inverse of covered call); purpose unclear but probably offsetting another position.
