# Market Recap: Chewy, Nvidia, and GameStop Earnings Analysis

## Summary

Three major earnings events dominate the market: Chewy reports before market open on August 28th with unusual call volume (31,000 contracts on 30 Aug 32-strike calls); Nvidia reports after close with massive institutional positioning (tens of millions in deep ITM calls); GameStop continues its downtrend ahead of September 4th earnings with declining open interest and negative momentum indicators. The scale of options activity differs dramatically across these three, with Nvidia dwarfing the others in absolute dollar terms.

## Key takeaways

### Chewy (CHWY)
- **Volume expansion into earnings** [01:05]: Volume jumped from 4.8M (Aug 23) → 6M (Aug 26) → 7.8M (Aug 27), signaling increased positioning ahead of pre-market earnings release.
- **Unusual call concentration** [02:26]–[03:40]: 96,000 calls vs. 44,000 puts; 31,000 contracts in 30 Aug 32-strike calls alone. These 5,000-lot blocks historically associated with Roaring Kitty, though attribution remains speculative.
- **Possible Roaring Kitty fingerprints** [06:03]–[07:17]: End-of-day block trades (~$500k position) align with his historical behavior; however, 20-delta strikes are lighter than his typical 30-delta preference. Size is small relative to his known account capacity (~$25M+ remaining after Chewy entry).
- **Mixed historical earnings pattern** [02:26]: Chewy's post-earnings moves are unpredictable (gaps up/down across recent quarters); implied volatility often exceeded by realized moves, making directional earnings plays risky.
- **FTD cycle overlap possible** [11:29]: Options activity dominates, but concurrent failure-to-deliver cycles could amplify any move.

### Nvidia (NVDA)
- **Massive institutional positioning** [13:44]–[14:57]: Top single position is $10M; multiple positions in $20M+ range. Deep ITM calls (80-strike) hit at size, suggesting hedged long stock or directional bullish bet.
- **Scale disparity** [13:44]: Nvidia options volume dwarfs Chewy; positions don't drop below $1M until scrolling far down the chain. Tens of millions flowing daily.
- **Call-heavy bias** [16:17]: 1.4M calls vs. 886k puts today; 708k vs. 423k for upcoming expiration. Massive put hedging (16,850 × 90-strike puts) suggests large stock holders protecting through release.
- **Earnings timing advantage** [16:17]: Reports after close tomorrow, allowing intraday directional play without overnight gap risk.
- **Market concentration risk** [12:42]: Nvidia comprises ~10% of S&P 500, significant portion of Nasdaq; single stock can move entire market.

### GameStop (GME)
- **Continued downtrend** [18:42]–[21:07]: Lower lows; support at ~$20.35 (volume node + 200-day MA convergence). RSI below 50-line; 4-day EMA sloping negative.
- **Declining call/put ratio** [18:42]: 550k calls vs. 273k puts (away from typical 2:1 ratio); open interest contracting. Front expiration: 114k calls vs. 26k puts (still call-heavy but lower absolute volume).
- **Earnings date uncertainty** [18:42]: Scheduled for Sept 4, but implied volatility structure (78–96% for 13 Sept) suggests possible date shift. Hypothesis: earnings may move, but confirmation window closing (one week out).
- **Quiet positioning** [18:42]: Relative calm in options market; volume below 20-day average. Expect sideways action until catalyst or earnings confirmation.
