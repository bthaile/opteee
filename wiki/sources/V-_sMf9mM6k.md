# The simple documentation hack for better AI results #ai #productivity

## Summary

A documentation system for managing AI conversations using three core documents: a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) as the single source of truth, a Roadmap for session-specific goals and research, and a Claude MD file that the AI updates automatically. This approach scales context management and ensures consistency across extended AI interactions by offloading documentation maintenance to the AI itself.

## Key takeaways

- **Three-document system** [00:00]: Maintain an SOP (comprehensive reference), Roadmap (session goals and research tracking), and Claude MD (AI-generated summary) to structure AI conversations.
- **SOP as source of truth** [00:00]: The SOP serves as the canonical reference document, growing to thousands of lines as the system scales.
- **Roadmap for digestibility** [00:21]: Keep the Roadmap separate from the SOP to avoid bloat; use it for discrete session-level notes and research priorities.
- **Automated documentation updates** [00:43]: At the end of each chat, instruct Claude to review all three documents, make updates based on the session, and report changes for your review.
- **AI executes the documentation** [00:43]: Delegate the entire documentation maintenance process to the AI, reducing manual overhead.
