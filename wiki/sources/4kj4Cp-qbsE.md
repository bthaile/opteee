# Coverage Strangle Position Updates: GameStop & Bitcoin (iBit)

Eric runs live position updates on two [[covered-strangle]] trades: [[gme]] and [[btc]] (via [[ibit]]). - **GameStop position structure** [00:00–07:40]: 300 shares at $26.95; two 28-strike puts (21 Feb, 52 DTE); one 35-strike call (planning to add a second on Monday). Objective is to collect premium on calls while holding shares, not to be assigned. Plan to buy additional shares outright Monday if allocation allows.

- **iBit technical setup** [10:52–11:51]: Trading at $53.84, basing but showing lower highs (bearish signal). Holding support lows (positive). Waiting for break above point of control for bullish continuation. Bitcoin position provides lighter correlation hedge against [[qqq]], [[upro]], [[spy]] exposure.
- **Portfolio allocation philosophy** [06:32–08:35]: Increased GME allocation to $25k (average account size benchmark). Maintaining 50% utilization to preserve dry powder for scaling into 30–40% drawdowns or catching upside breakouts. Coverage strangle designed around buy-and-hold core, not aggressive trading.
