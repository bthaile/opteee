# Portfolio Turnover MOS, BAC, KR, INTC - Small Stacks Pt14

## Summary

Eric reviews tactical adjustments made to the Small Stacks portfolio ahead of the 2020 election, closing the Mosaic short-call position for ~34% premium capture and establishing new exposure in Bank of America puts. He discusses the one-year intended duration of the series, rebalances diagonal spreads in Kroger and Intel by rolling short calls to earlier expirations for improved theta sensitivity, and notes the portfolio's realized P&L of ~$1,000 (17% return) with $243 in premium still outstanding.

## Key takeaways

- **Portfolio duration & intent** [00:00–00:52]: Eric clarifies the Small Stacks series is planned for approximately one year (through ~September 2021) to balance educational value with personal time allocation; open to feedback on timeline.
- **Mosaic exit pre-earnings** [02:07–04:00]: Closed short-call position for 59¢, capturing ~34% of premium; exited before earnings to avoid post-earnings volatility despite favorable setup, demonstrating purposeful exposure management.
- **Bank of America short puts** [04:00–04:57]: Initiated 20-Nov $24 puts at $1.22 premium; chose BAC over XLF despite similar price levels due to superior premium return relative to buying power; early in trade lifecycle with gap-up risk noted.
- **Kroger diagonal scaling** [05:46–07:03]: Sold 18-Dec $35 calls at 65¢ (30-delta) on existing LEAPS; maintains 1:2 ratio (uncapped upside); willing to scale to 1:1 ratio on further upside to exit entire position at profit; unrealized loss on LEAPS (~$380) offset by $288 realized profit on short calls.
- **Intel call roll for theta** [07:22–09:45]: Closed 20-Nov $47 calls (sold 1.65¢, bought 74¢) for $91 realized profit; rolled to new 20-Nov $47 calls at 56¢ to capture higher daily theta despite lower total remaining premium; dividend ex-date (Nov 5) creates additional roll opportunity.
- **Portfolio metrics** [10:08–10:55]: Net unrealized P&L ~$5,500 (mostly Kroger LEAPS drag); 80% buying-power utilization; $1,000 realized profit (17% return since Sept 1); $243 premium outstanding (~4% return on account).
- **Volatility positioning ahead** [11:20–12:15]: Monitoring for post-election volatility spikes; considering VXX calls if uncertainty emerges; may execute via margin account futures and mirror in Small Stacks account.
