# Mastering Options Trading  The Secret to Consistent Gains

## Summary

This video emphasizes the foundational principle that consistent options trading success depends on executing a repeatable process with positive expected value, not on the outcome of individual trades. The core thesis is that traders should focus on finding high-conviction setups, following rules rigorously, and trusting the process over time rather than chasing salvage trades or obsessing over daily P&L.

## Key takeaways

- **Process over outcome** [00:00–00:16]: The perfect options trade is one that follows a proven process yielding positive expected return, not one that automatically moves in your favor or "looks beautiful." Success is measured by adherence to process, not individual trade results.
- **Avoid salvage trades** [00:00]: Do not break rules to rescue a losing position; this destroys edge and introduces emotional decision-making.
- **Stack strength** [00:00]: Build positions by finding setups so compelling you "can't say no"—high-conviction entries with favorable risk/reward.
- **Be an outlier** [00:16]: Consistent gains come from being willing to think and act differently from the crowd, trusting your edge and plan.

## Notable quotes

> "It's not about whether or not the trade makes money it's about following a process that we know yields positive expected return over time and continuing to do that"
