# Trading Earnings, VIX, CPI, Reddit AMA | Live Stock Market Analysis

## Summary

Live market analysis covering macro context (oil–semis–energy relationships), deflationary CPI/PPI data shifting Fed rate expectations, VIX term structure dynamics, earnings strategy mechanics with focus on risk premium through earnings events, and community Q&A on technical indicators and signal design. Key theme: matching signals to profit mechanisms rather than using indicators in isolation.

## Key takeaways

- **Macro setup & commodity relationships** [16:40–18:14]: Oil–semis–energy–SPY relationships have expanded; external shocks (Iran conflict) create novel market dislocations; persistence or reversal of these relationships are tradeable.
- **CPI/PPI deflation surprise** [18:14–19:48]: June PPI came in at −1.3% (vs. est. −0.2%), June CPI at −0.4% (vs. est. −0.2%); massive repricing of Fed expectations.
- **Fed rate expectations flip** [19:48–22:37]: Probability of July/September rate hike dropped from ~42% to 16% in one day; historical shift from May's 375–400 bps scenario now at 16% probability; no-move scenario now overwhelmingly priced.
- **VIX term structure compression** [30:08–34:25]: VIX spot at ~15.67 (55.6% off one-year highs); VIX 1-day at 9.5; front-end dropping significantly while 30-day and 3-month mirroring; relative value trade opportunity between 30-day and 3-month VIX.
- **Earnings risk premium mechanics** [24:07–28:50]: Hedgers buy puts for downside protection → premium sellers compensated; variance risk premium skews toward put side; structure accordingly (e.g., 2:1 put-to-call ratio or higher-delta puts in strangle). Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, Citigroup, Morgan Stanley all beat with minimal moves—successful cycle. Upcoming: Google (22nd), Meta (29th), Apple (30th), Amazon (30th); Google and Meta historically move more; Microsoft and Amazon move less relative to priced vol.
- **Microsoft earnings case study** [36:02–37:31]: 5 of past 8 earnings outside expected move; move size ~1.1×; past 4 RP −0.8, past 8 RP −1.1; despite high IVP (100%), not attractive for short-vol earnings play given current pricing; dynamic, not static, approach required.
- **Signal design & profit mechanisms** [52:31–01:01:34]: Indicators depend on timeframe and profit mechanism; match signals to the specific edge you're trading (e.g., momentum ribbon for long-drift strategies). Build a research repository to understand signal–profit-mechanism overlays; signals are relative, not magical.
- **Covered strangle / coverage triangle example** [56:08–58:42]: Used to optimize dollar-cost-average strategy; momentum factors determine allocation and long-shares vs. short-puts mix; moving-average ribbon (10, 20, 50, 110, 200) chosen via research, not randomly; profit driver is positive drift + long momentum.
- **Upcoming workshops** [01:03:01–01:04:20]: Tomorrow (skill development): conducting research; Monday 20th (Pro+ workshop): evaluating research results; Saturday live stream: O-Talk deep dive; free 30-min 1-on-1 available.
