# Don't forget to have a plan! The market does actually have down days!

## Summary
A brief market reminder that equities move in both directions, not just upward. The session emphasizes the importance of having a pre-planned approach, particularly for traders holding short positions in the Nasdaq, to avoid panic-driven decisions during downside moves.

## Key takeaways

- **Markets move both ways** [00:00] — the market does not only go up; down days are a normal part of market structure and should be anticipated.
- **Plan before the move** [00:00] — traders, especially those short the Nasdaq, need a predetermined plan to manage positions during downside volatility.
- **Avoid permanence bias** [00:00] — a down day does not mean the market will continue falling indefinitely; emotional discipline requires trusting your plan rather than reacting to intraday moves.
