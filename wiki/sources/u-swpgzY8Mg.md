# Modeling an Options Trading for Beginners #optionstrading

## Summary

Eric introduces the **outlier trade process**, a systematic framework distinct from the outlier strategy process that traders follow before placing any trade. The core emphasis is on step three: generating a thesis and modeling the trade, which allows options traders to add complexity in service of tailored, idea-specific positioning.

## Key takeaways

- **The outlier trade process is a pre-trade checklist** [00:00] — a specific sequence Eric follows before every trade execution, separate from the broader strategy process.
- **Step three is thesis generation and trade modeling** [00:23] — the critical juncture where options traders convert an idea into a structured position.
- **Options complexity is a feature, not a bug** [00:23] — the added complexity of options allows for tailored-fit positioning that aligns precisely with your thesis.

## Notable quotes
