# Welcome to The Options Trench

## Summary

Eric and Chris introduce *The Options Trench*, a new educational series designed to demystify options trading for both new and experienced traders. The show aims to answer practical, community-sourced questions about options mechanics and strategy through the lens of real trading experience rather than formulaic instruction. Episodes will release weekly on YouTube and Spotify.

## Key takeaways

- **Series mission** [00:02]: The Options Trench exists because the options world is nuanced; many traders think they're doing things right but execute incorrectly, and the goal is to facilitate learning for traders at all levels.
- **Eric's origin story** [00:02–02:07]: Started trading in 2007 as a high school student after learning about compound interest; initially traded options "in quite literally all of the wrong ways humanly possible" until a Facebook mentor taught him to treat trading as a business with a written plan; self-taught through undergrad/grad stats and Marine Corps service; founded Outlier Trading to share knowledge.
- **Chris's background** [03:15–04:30]: Floor trader at SIG for 8 years, then ran a market-making operation on the NYSE trading energy, commodity, and precious-metals options for ~4 years; later ran a commodity relative-value volatility trading business (options on futures) for a venture fund in San Francisco.
- **Teaching philosophy** [05:29–06:36]: Chris describes himself as a "mathematical hard gainer" (not naturally gifted at math like many option traders); believes learning difficult material makes you a better teacher because you know where people get stuck; compares it to learning basketball from the shortest NBA player rather than LeBron James.
- **The options landscape problem** [07:35–08:30]: Much internet options content feels like "house flipper infomercials"—people want to believe it's easy, which spreads wildly but builds communities that churn; good information + credit for the work required can help traders improve long-term.
- **Chris's respect for self-taught traders** [08:30]: Admires Eric and others who succeed without formal prop-firm training; sees self-taught success as "very, very impressive."
- **Format and cadence** [09:34–11:31]: The show answers community-sourced questions; hosts will release episodes weekly on YouTube and Spotify; email: theoptionstrench@gmail.com.
