# What are Delta Bands

## Summary

Delta bands are a rebalancing framework for short premium positions that maintains a target delta range rather than holding to max profit. By rebalancing when delta drifts beyond a defined band (e.g., ±10 deltas from a 30-delta short put), traders capture additional upside participation while managing downside risk dynamically.

## Key takeaways

- **Delta band concept**: Set a target delta (e.g., 30) and rebalance when the underlying moves enough to shift delta by ±10 [00:00]
- **Upside capture**: As the underlying rises and delta decreases (30 → 20), rebalancing back to 30 delta recaptures profit potential that would otherwise be left on the table [00:29]
- **Downside symmetry**: The inverse applies on downside moves; tighter rebalance bands (10 delta) capture more upside participation than waiting for 50% max profit [00:51]
- **Participation vs. max profit**: Delta bands trade the certainty of max profit for higher expected return by reducing the "lack of participation" in favorable moves [00:51]
