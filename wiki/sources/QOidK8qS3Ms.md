# The Only Way to Quantify Market Effects

## Summary

Eric outlines the core challenge of the Outlier Strategy Process: identifying profit mechanisms and then rigorously measuring, detecting, predicting, and maintaining exposure to them. He emphasizes that confidence in a trading edge cannot be faked—it must be built through direct observation and measurement of repeatable patterns over time.

## Key takeaways

- **The two hardest steps in trading:** finding profit mechanisms and then quantifying your exposure to them [00:00–00:28]
- **Replicability is non-negotiable:** you must be able to reproduce your own results through research and testing; confidence cannot be borrowed from others [00:28–00:51]
- **Confidence builds through observation:** repeated measurement and direct observation of patterns over time is the only path to genuine conviction [00:51]
