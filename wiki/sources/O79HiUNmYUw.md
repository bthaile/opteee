# Market Update: Tech Leadership, Sector Breakdown, and Fed Uncertainty

## Summary

The S&P 500 is consolidating near all-time highs with a striking divergence: technology (led by Nvidia at ~$143.71) is rallying while real estate, healthcare, and staples are declining. Fed rate-cut expectations have collapsed—the probability of a 50 bps cut is now zero, and no-cut odds have risen to ~13%. Key economic data (jobless claims, PMI, consumer sentiment) and earnings releases this week will likely determine whether the market breaks higher or consolidates further.
