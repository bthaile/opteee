# Simplified Covered Strangle

## Summary

This video presents a streamlined approach to executing a covered strangle strategy, emphasizing simplicity and mechanical execution. The core principle is setting up the trade so that minimal monitoring is required between entry and expiration, allowing the trader to return only after expiration to assess results and plan the next steps.

## Key takeaways

- **Simplicity as a feature** [00:00] — The strategy should be executable in under 60 seconds on your platform; complexity is the enemy of consistent execution.
- **Set and forget mechanics** [00:00] — Once the covered strangle is established, you should not need to manage it mid-week or mid-month; the outcome is determined by expiration.
- **Post-expiration review** [00:00] — Return to the platform only after expiration to evaluate what happened and determine the next steps in the strategy.
- **Mechanical discipline** [00:00] — When structured this way, external market noise and intraday volatility become irrelevant to the trade outcome.
