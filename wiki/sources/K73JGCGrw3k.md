# Never Stop Trading Tom Sosnoff's Passion | The Outlier Podcast

## Summary

Tom Sosnoff discusses his enduring passion for trading and why he has no intention of stepping away from the markets. Despite changes in his public profile, he remains actively engaged in trading daily, emphasizing that the drive to trade is intrinsic to who he is rather than a temporary pursuit.

## Key takeaways

- **Active daily trading commitment** [00:00] — Sosnoff executed approximately 77 trades on the day of recording, describing it as a "slow day," underscoring his consistent market engagement regardless of market conditions.
- **Trading as identity, not hobby** [00:00–00:29] — He frames trading not as an optional activity but as a core part of his identity: "That's what I do. I love it. Never stop."
- **Psychological addiction to markets** [00:29] — Acknowledges that trading is an addiction-like compulsion for which "there's no rehab," suggesting the psychological pull of market participation is fundamental and inescapable for him.

## Notable quotes

> "I'm never going to stop trading, by the way. That's what I do. I love it."

> "I don't think there's a rehab for this one."
