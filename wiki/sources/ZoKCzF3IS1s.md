# Build the habit #optionstrading

## Summary

A foundational lesson on establishing consistent investing discipline before optimizing strategy. The core message: begin investing immediately with whatever capital you can afford, prioritizing habit formation over waiting for a large lump sum. Once the discipline is established, optimization of approach becomes meaningful.

## Key takeaways

- **Start now, not later** [00:00] — Don't delay investing until you reach an arbitrary dollar threshold; open a brokerage account and begin immediately with whatever amount you can afford.
- **Habit precedes optimization** [00:30] — The primary goal in early investing is building the behavioral pattern of regular investing, not maximizing returns or strategy sophistication.
- **Index ETFs as entry point** [00:30] — Most people default to index ETFs in retirement and primary brokerage accounts; this is a sensible starting vehicle while habit-building.
- **Applies to wealth levers broadly** [00:00] — The principle extends to the three primary wealth drivers: saving, investing, and income expansion.
