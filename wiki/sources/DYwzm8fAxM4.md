# Tesla Stock Split News #shorts

## Summary
Tesla's board approved a stock split via stock dividend to be submitted to shareholders at an upcoming annual meeting, likely in late 2022. The mechanism differs from traditional cash dividends: shareholders receive additional shares rather than cash. The split ratio has not yet been announced.

## Key takeaways
- Tesla filed with the SEC announcing board approval of a stock split via stock dividend [00:00]
- The split will be presented to shareholders at the annual meeting, expected later in 2022 (historically held in June, but recent meetings in September–October) [00:00]
- Split ratio will be announced closer to the shareholder meeting date [00:22]
- Tesla's previous stock split was in August 2020 at a 5-for-1 ratio [00:22]
- A stock dividend split differs from cash dividends: shareholders receive additional shares instead of cash [00:22]
