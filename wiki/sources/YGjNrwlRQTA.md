# Watchlist Development & Opening Positions - Small Stacks Pt2

## Summary

Eric walks through his watchlist-building process for a small $5k account and presents two initial short-put positions: selling a $125 September 17 put on Mosaic (MOS) for 53¢ and an $18 September 18 put on DXC for 55¢. Both trades target ~31% return on invested capital, with a combined portfolio allocation of ~70%, balancing sector exposure (IT via DXC, materials via MOS) while maintaining at least two positions to avoid concentration risk.

## Key takeaways

- **Watchlist constraints for small accounts** [00:22–01:32]: Prefer S&P 500 constituents with weekly options and ≥2% yield, but accept trade-offs (e.g., no weeklies) when capital is limited; distinguish between "prefer" (small account) and hard rules (normal account).
- **Sector rotation hybrid approach** [01:51–02:17]: Screen top 3–5 performing sectors over 3-month and 30-day windows, but allow exceptions for strong individual performers outside the top tier.
- **Position sizing discipline** [02:43–03:07]: Target stock prices ≤$50–60 to maintain at least two concurrent positions in a $5k account; avoid single-ticker concentration.
- **Initial trades rationale** [03:33–04:26]: DXC provides IT sector exposure; Mosaic offers materials exposure with stable price action post-drop; both pass preliminary fundamental and technical checks.
- **Execution plan** [04:26–05:00]: Establish DXC immediately; defer Mosaic pending price-level reaction; remain flexible to pivot if market conditions warrant.
- **Portfolio metrics** [05:22–05:49]: Combined ROIC ~31% on the two trades; ~70% of account deployed (constrained by cash-securing requirements); acceptable risk-adjusted return for the capital committed.

## Notable quotes

> "I definitely want some IT exposure which is what DXC satisfies for me and then Mosaic I like because if we pull up their fundamentals from a just one materials perspective I don't feel super passionate about that per se but when I look at something that's been fairly stable after the drop I definitely think Mosaic is there."
