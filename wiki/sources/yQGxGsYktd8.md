# Set Your PnL Goals BEFORE Entering Your Trades

## Summary

P&L management and exit targets must be determined *before* entering a trade, not during it. The research, planning, and decision-making work should be complete before you click the button to enter; execution itself should be the easiest part of the process.

## Key takeaways

- **Pre-trade planning is non-negotiable** [00:00] — Set P&L goals and exit criteria before entry every single time; doing this after entry means you're already behind.
- **The work happens before execution** [00:24] — All research and analysis should be complete before you enter the trade; clicking the button should be the easiest part if you've done the work properly.
- **Avoid reactive decision-making** [00:00] — Many traders ask what to do *while already in* a trade, indicating they skipped the planning phase; this leads to emotional, bumpy trading.

## Notable quotes

> "The work is done before you enter. When you enter a trade, when you finally click the button, that genuinely should be the easiest part of the transaction going forward if you do a good job."
