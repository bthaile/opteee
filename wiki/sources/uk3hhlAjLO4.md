# SIMPLICITY #investing #stockmarket #trading

## Summary

A foundational lesson on the power of simplicity in trading: price, volume, moving averages, and support/resistance provide the core toolkit needed for consistent results. The speaker argues that chasing exotic indicators or "secret sauce" strategies is a distraction; the real edge lies in mastering obvious mechanics and avoiding noise.

## Key takeaways

- **Price, volume, moving averages, and support/resistance get you most of the way there** [00:00] — these four elements form a complete trading foundation without exotic add-ons.
- **There is no secret indicator that guarantees 100% success** [00:21] — if such an indicator existed, everyone would already use it, so stop searching for it.
- **Simplicity has beauty and efficacy** [00:00] — the longer you trade, the more you realize that obvious mechanics work; don't tinker endlessly chasing marginal improvements.
- **Avoid noise and tail-chasing** [00:00] — resist the urge to layer complexity; simple things get you "pretty close" without the distraction.

## Notable quotes

> "Keep the chart simple. There is beauty and simplicity, and the longer you do this, the more you realize the efficacy behind what seems to be blatantly obvious."

> "There is no secret sauce, no unknown indicator that leads to a hundred percent success rates—because it doesn't exist."
