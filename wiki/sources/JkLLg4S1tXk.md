# DJT's Election Surge: Analyzing Market Momentum

## Summary

DJT stock is trading near all-time highs with extreme implied volatility (300%+) and IV percentile at 98%, functioning as a pure election-outcome speculation vehicle decoupled from broad market beta. The stock exhibits massive upside skew in near-term probability distributions despite fundamental stagnation, driven by binary event risk ahead of the election and heavy unusual options activity across both calls and puts. Volume has expanded consistently since early October, with expected monthly move around 88%, making this a high-volatility hedge and directional play for portfolio managers positioning around election outcomes.

## Key takeaways

- **Decoupling from market beta** [01:08]: DJT has broken correlation with SPY, QQQ, and IWM over the past two days, rising while broad indices roll over—a clear signal of pure election-outcome speculation rather than market-driven movement.
- **Prediction market context** [02:21]: PolyMarket shows ~64% Trump / 36% Harris probability; polls show Harris 48.2% vs Trump 47%, suggesting truth lies between prediction markets and traditional polling.
- **Volume expansion and lockup expiration** [03:36]: 20-day average volume now ~37.6M shares, 5-day ~43M; lockup period expired with modest selling pressure, indicating sustained speculative demand.
- **Extreme implied volatility metrics** [04:50]: IV at 300%+ (down from earlier), IV percentile 98%, ATR 5-day 7.45%, 20-day 8.3%, expected monthly move ~88%—binary event pricing.
- **Skewed options structure** [06:00]: November 8 expiration shows 2,783 calls vs 35,000 puts; November 15 shows 156 calls vs 152 puts—heavy put buying and mixed directional positioning.
- **Unusual whale activity** [06:00]: Large trades observed on Oct 23 including $2.3M+ in 2026 calls at 37 strike; heavy premium volume across both sides suggests portfolio hedging alongside directional bets.
- **Near-term upside skew** [09:26]: Probability analysis shows massive upside skew in next week, with ~16% probability of touch to $60–$61 (one standard deviation), despite downside skew beyond 60 days.
- **Positioning rationale** [08:24]: Small speculative position held based on large average daily range breakouts; uncertainty on how far rally extends before election.
