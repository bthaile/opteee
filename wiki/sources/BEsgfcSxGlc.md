# Brutal Honesty in Trading Success

## Summary

Self-awareness and brutal honesty are foundational to trading success. The ability to take accountability for mistakes, overcome ego-driven blame-shifting, and understand your natural trading propensities is essential. Building systematic checks into your process allows you to counteract your inherent weaknesses—such as moving too fast—and maintain discipline.

## Key takeaways

- **Self-awareness is critical** [00:00]: You must learn to be brutally honest with yourself about your trading performance and mistakes; many traders fail because they cannot take accountability and instead cast blame.
- **Identify your natural propensities** [00:18]: Understand your trading style and instincts (e.g., moving fast) and recognize the downsides (e.g., missing important details).
- **Build process checks to counteract weaknesses** [00:38]: Insert deliberate friction or verification steps into your trading system to force yourself to slow down and avoid costly oversights.
