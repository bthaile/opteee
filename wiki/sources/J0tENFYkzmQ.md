# Spotting BS #optionstrading

## Summary

Eric identifies a key red flag for spotting trading grifters and shills: those who exclusively highlight wins and dollar amounts while ignoring losses. Genuine traders focus on losses as learning opportunities and process gaps, whereas those with something to sell fixate on flashy profit narratives. This distinction separates authentic traders from those peddling false promises.

## Key takeaways

- **Loss-focused analysis is the mark of a serious trader** [00:23] — real traders discuss losses extensively because they reveal process gaps and problems; this is how traders help each other improve.
- **Beware of win-only narratives** [00:00] — if someone only talks about their wins or dollar sizes of profits (e.g., "$5,000 in 14 milliseconds"), they likely have something to sell.
- **Fast money is possible but not the focus of legitimate traders** [00:45] — while you can make money quickly, traders who emphasize speed and size typically use that narrative as a sales hook rather than a genuine trading principle.

## Notable quotes
