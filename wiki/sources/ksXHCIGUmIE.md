# Transform your trading #optionstrading #trading #stockmarket

## Summary

The core transformation is mechanical: define both a profit target and a loss target *before* entering any trade. This simple pre-trade discipline forces traders to confront the reality of losses and removes emotion from exit decisions. The caveat is flexibility at profit targets—you may scale out or trail a stop—but loss targets are non-negotiable.

## Key takeaways

- **Define profit and loss targets before entry [00:00]** — the single easiest overnight improvement to trading discipline; most traders skip this because it forces them to acknowledge losing trades.
- **Loss targets are hard stops [00:46]** — when a trade hits your loss target, exit immediately; this is non-negotiable.
- **Profit targets allow flexibility [00:27]** — you may take partial profits, trail a stop, or leave some on; it's not binary. But loss targets are binary.
- **Acknowledge losing trades as inevitable [00:00]** — our emotional desire to avoid thinking about losses doesn't change whether they happen; they're part of the process.

## Notable quotes

> "Define a profit target and a loss target, pick those two points before you enter the trade—that's it, really simple."

> "When something hits your loss target, got to get out."
