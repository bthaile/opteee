# INSIDE Roaring Kitty's CHWY Position & GME Lawsuit

## Summary

Roaring Kitty disclosed a ~9.1 million share position in Chewy (CHWY) on June 24, representing approximately $225 million in capital—a dramatic expansion from his prior $6+ million position. Simultaneously, a class-action lawsuit alleging pump-and-dump manipulation was filed June 28 and voluntarily dismissed without prejudice on July 1, raising questions about legal strategy and timing. GameStop options show heavy call skew with meaningful position-building in the July 19 expiration, while Chewy options analysis suggests the capital deployment likely involved a combination of share sales, covered-call premium collection, and possibly other trading activity rather than options-only leverage.
