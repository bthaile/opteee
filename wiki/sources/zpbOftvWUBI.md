# GME Stock Update & Options Analysis

## Summary

Current GME price action shows a five-day downtrend hovering near $21, with a volume node support zone at ~$20.50. Open interest remains heavily skewed toward calls (650k vs. 284k puts), with notable concentration in October 18 expiration (273k calls vs. 92k puts). Volatility across the entire chain is muted—unusually so, with no strikes above 100% IV—suggesting a potential setup for significant movement. The host plans to conduct systematic historical analysis of options activity patterns across 2021–2023 to understand market structure before and after Roaring Kitty's return.
