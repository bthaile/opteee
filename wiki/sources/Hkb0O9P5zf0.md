### Evergreen mechanics

- **Rolling options is not magic [17:44–31:43]**: Rolling = closing one trade + opening another. Case study: trader rolled 12 GLD covered calls from 31-DTE 361 strike (1.0 delta) to 16-Jan 369 strike (0.88 delta), incurring net debit. Break-even on new position: 26.15. Key lesson: track cash impact and net liquidating value separately; rolling doesn't automatically credit account.
- **Position sizing around earnings [08:52]**: Discord member building earnings vol model; reminder that 3 years of casual options trading = beginner level. Feedback loops and tracking earnings trades systematically improve edge.
- **Covered strangle outperformance on GME [56:18–58:03]**: Covered strangle up 22% YTD vs GME down 30.5%—outperformance of 50.8%. Ability to navigate volatility spikes (short puts + short calls) captures earnings moves while hedging directional risk.
