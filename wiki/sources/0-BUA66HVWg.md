# GameStop Option Expiry & Massive Put Buying

## Summary

GameStop closed above $30 on December 27 expiration day, with the stock holding steady above this psychological level since December 23. Massive call volume (247k calls vs. 56k puts) concentrated in the $32 strike forced significant dealer hedging activity, supporting price action. Far-dated put buying (2026 expiration, $98–$450k blocks) suggests speculative directional positioning or portfolio hedging by a likely single entity.

## Key takeaways

- **Price holds above $30 handle** [01:13]: GME broke through the anticipated $30 resistance and has remained steady above it since Dec 23, with today's candle showing large wicks but small body—indicating price battle but no rejection.
- **RSI 61–55, 7-day VWAP still upsloping** [02:32]: Momentum indicators remain constructive; volatility at 62% near-term.
- **Volume node floor at $28–$37 range** [03:52]: One-month transaction level now acts as fundamental value floor; $30 becoming the next handle to beat.
- **Massive call concentration on $32 strike** [05:05]: 24,700 calls vs. 7,960 puts on $32 expiry; forces direct dealer hedging in open market to cover assignment risk.
- **January expiration is "spicy"** [05:05]: 428,000 calls (half of all open calls) in Jan 17 expiration; 62k calls vs. 14k puts on Jan 3.
- **Call skew emerges only in April and beyond** [07:27]: Near-term (Jan–Feb) shows balanced or put-heavy skew; April 17 onwards shows heavy call skew, indicating market pricing larger upside moves further out.
- **Far-dated put buying: $98–$450k blocks in 2026** [08:42]: Multiple large put purchases at offer in 120 expiration (2026); likely same entity; speculative directional play rather than locate acquisition (position too small, too far out).
- **Max pain at $29.50** [10:01]: Stock closed well within max pain; dealer hedging activity likely supported price above this level.
- **Relative strength despite broad market down** [11:09]: GME down only 2.39% while broader market declined significantly; inside-day candle indicates relative strength.
