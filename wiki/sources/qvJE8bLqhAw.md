# Purpose of PLANS

## Summary
Eric articulates the core philosophy behind his trading approach: systematic analysis of trade variables to identify optimal decision-making. Rather than relying on intuition or mimicry, he emphasizes the unglamorous work of understanding what actually drives profitability across different strategies and market conditions.

## Key takeaways

- **Systematic variable analysis** [00:00–00:20]: The defining edge is willingness to do the analytical work others avoid—mapping which variables matter most in any given trade or strategy.
- **Optimization through understanding** [00:20]: Once variables are identified and understood, they can be optimized; this process-first approach separates consistent traders from reactive ones.
- **Psychological honesty** [00:00]: Acknowledging that all traders are subject to the same biases and impulses; the difference lies in the discipline to analyze rather than guess.
