# SIMPLE Discipline HACK #investing #mindset

## Summary

A brief behavioral technique for building trading discipline: deliberately delay gratification on small daily decisions (eating, drinking, breaks) by 2–5 minutes to develop an internal mechanism that transfers to trading decisions. The practice trains impulse control and intentional decision-making.

## Key takeaways

- **Delay-of-gratification drill** [00:00]: Pick any small daily action (beverage, lunch, break) and impose a 2–5 minute waiting period before executing it. Make the delay conscious and deliberate.
- **Transfer to trading** [00:22]: Developing this internal discipline mechanism in everyday life directly strengthens trading psychology and decision-making under pressure.
- **Applies across life domains**: The same impulse-control skill generalizes beyond trading to general life discipline and intentional action.

## Notable quotes

> "Make yourself wait two minutes, three minutes, five minutes. Pick a time. We start to develop a powerful internal mechanism that transfers over beautifully to trading and the rest of life."
