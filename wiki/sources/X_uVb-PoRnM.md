# GME Earnings & Flow Analysis | Stock Market News

## Summary

Eric reviews broad market action on SPX/QQQ showing a delicate technical setup with declining breadth despite index gains, then pivots to GameStop's Q2 earnings release. GME reported positive net income ($14.8M vs. prior-year loss), $4.24B in cash, and declining sales, closing ~10% below earnings. The analysis emphasizes the need for management to articulate capital deployment strategy and examines unusual options flow and block trades in GME.

## Key takeaways

### Dated market read (2024-09-10)

- **SPX/QQQ technical setup**: Market gapped up, crashed, then rallied from ~10:00–11:00 ET; percentage of S&P 500 stocks above 50-day MA declined despite index gain, signaling narrow breadth [00:22–01:00]
- **QQQ trend structure**: Higher lows maintained but lower low created; price level critical—break below confirms downtrend; RSI crossing EMA shows near-term momentum shift but still near 50% midpoint [01:21–04:19]
- **Tech positioning**: Eric not adding tech exposure yet; watching for rally above key resistance with light volume; smooth sailing above that zone [02:51–03:38]

### Evergreen mechanics

- **Breadth divergence**: Index strength without breadth confirmation is a warning signal; monitor % of stocks above key moving averages [01:00]
- **Volume profile analysis**: Identify volume nodes and resistance; smooth volume profile above resistance suggests easier rally path [03:15–03:38]
- **Money flow + momentum**: Positive MFI slope + RSI crossing EMA = near-term bullish signal, but context matters (RSI at 50% is neutral) [03:56–04:19]

### GameStop earnings analysis (2024-09-10)

- **Stock reaction**: Closed at $23.45 post-earnings, trading ~$21.60 at time of recording (−10% move); typical for GME earnings volatility [04:42–05:10]
- **Financials snapshot**: Net sales $0.789B (down from $1.64B prior period); net income $14.8M (vs. prior-year loss); stockholders' equity jumped from $1.27B to $4.38B [05:57–10:21]
- **Cash position**: $4.24B in cash; Eric emphasizes lack of forward guidance on capital deployment—cash sitting idle doesn't drive business value [06:22–07:44]
- **Segment performance**: Hardware/accessory sales down; software down; collectibles down; positive net sales but contraction across categories [12:44–13:45]
- **Missing catalyst**: No management commentary on cash use; Eric notes Cohen likely has plans but keeping strategy private [06:22–07:44]

### Options & flow analysis

- **Large block trades**: 5,000-lot blocks recurring across multiple days (Sept 3–10); not unique to any single trader; larger trades (up to ~100K) also active [16:17–20:25]
- **Dark pool liquidity**: 2M+ premium trades executing; blocks of 10K–100K moving without market impact; indicates deep liquidity [19:10–20:00]
- **Timing patterns**: Some 5,000-lot blocks clustered within seconds (e.g., 07:07, 07:10, 07:32); tight clustering suggests possible single entity, but day-long distribution suggests multiple participants [21:07–21:54]
- **Earnings call options**: 99-strike calls for $28K (Friday expiry) underwater post-earnings; Eric filters for single-leg trades to avoid spread noise [15:50–16:17]
