# Become the Tom Brady of Options Trading #optionstrading

## Summary

This video reframes options trading success through the lens of process consistency rather than single-trade outcomes. Drawing parallels to Tom Brady's championship methodology, the core argument is that sustained profitability comes from executing a repeatable, well-designed process reliably over many occurrences—not from obsessing over any individual trade result.

## Key takeaways

- **Shift focus from outcome to process** [00:00] — Worrying about a single trade's result causes you to miss the bigger picture; excellence in trading, like in sports, comes from consistent execution of a proven system.
- **Build a baked-in process** [00:31] — Tom Brady's success wasn't about one perfect throw; it was about preparation before the event, focus during execution, and a repeatable methodology applied across hundreds of games.
- **Consistency beats perfection** [00:54] — The goal is not to do it well once, but to do it well repeatedly; this shift in mindset is foundational to long-term profitability.

## Notable quotes
