# Market Recap | mpox, SIGA, CSCO, GME

## Summary

A market-update covering three key catalysts: SIGA's 26.9% surge on monkeypox public health emergency declaration and antiviral demand; Cisco's earnings beat (87¢ vs. 77¢ estimate) with 7% workforce reduction and modest gap-up; and Alibaba pre-earnings positioning ahead of morning report. Broader market shows divergence across indices (Russell down, S&P up, Nasdaq mixed) with unusual breadth signals and elevated repositioning volume. GameStop remains in a sideways holding pattern with low volume and front-month options concentration.

## Key takeaways

### Dated market read (2024-08-14)

- **SIGA breakout on mpox catalyst** [00:22–04:40]: SIGA up 26.9% on WHO declaration of monkeypox as public health emergency; company produces antiviral (tecovirimat). Volume surged to ~13M (vs. 1.2M 20-day avg). Breakout above $10 resistance triggered nibbling; ratio call diagonals used for illiquid options. Early-stage event-driven trade; deadlier variant emergence is key watch.

- **Cisco earnings beat, workforce cut** [05:23–07:20]: Beat EPS (87¢ vs. 77¢ est.), revenue ~$13.64B in line. Stock gapped up ~$3 after-hours (~$48 vs. $45 close). 7% workforce reduction typical cost-cutting ahead of next quarter. Slight bullish skew on position due to upside risk; underperformance vs. AI-driven peers noted.

- **Alibaba pre-earnings setup** [08:12–08:57]: Reports tomorrow morning; closed down 2% after-hours (~$80). Parabolic chop around prior earnings; used as China/broader-economy proxy. Not actively trading; monitoring for macro signal.

- **Broad market divergence & breadth warning** [09:17–14:25]: Russell 2000 down, S&P 500 up, Nasdaq mixed—unusual divergence. Net highs/lows on QQQ flat (56 today vs. 59 yesterday) despite buy-volume dominance (65%), but gross highs +21 and gross lows −20 day-over-day. Sell volume massive (17.38M vs. 7.8M buy) signals large-fund repositioning. Rate-cut expectations (100% priced) not lifting small caps. Cautious stance: shorter-term velocity trades, earnings plays, zero-DTE; avoiding drift until setup clarifies.

- **GameStop sideways grind** [14:46–18:01]: Trading $21.54–$21.90 over 5 days; volume anemic (3.46M today vs. 8.3M 20-day avg). Front-month (16 Aug) expiration heavily built (295K calls, 106K puts); 23 Aug and 30 Aug quiet (41K/11K and 37K/10K). Earnings IV not yet elevated. Strategy: holding shares, selling calls against position for theta support. Out-of-money call volatility still attractive for premium selling.

### Evergreen mechanics

- **Event-driven catalyst trading**: Monkeypox declaration → SIGA antiviral demand is textbook tradable event; watch for deadlier-variant emergence as escalation risk.
- **Earnings gap management**: Cisco's $3 gap-up manageable; bullish skew (fear upside more than downside) typical for beaten estimates.
- **Breadth divergence as regime signal**: Flat net highs/lows + massive sell volume despite buy dominance = large-fund repositioning; small-cap underperformance despite rate-cut pricing is anomalous.
- **Options liquidity trade-off**: SIGA options illiquid but tradable for retail; ratio diagonals viable for small size.
- **Theta decay in sideways markets**: GameStop's range-bound action makes covered-call selling (against long shares) efficient capital use.
