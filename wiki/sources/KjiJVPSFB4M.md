# IBIT vs BITO - What's Better?

## Summary

A direct comparison of two Bitcoin ETFs: IBIT (spot Bitcoin) versus BITO (Bitcoin futures). IBIT holds actual Bitcoin and significantly outperforms BITO due to lower expense ratios and the structural cost disadvantage of trading spot futures contracts. The host trades IBIT and uses it as the underlying for covered strangle positions.

## Key takeaways

- **IBIT holds actual Bitcoin; BITO trades Bitcoin futures contracts** [00:00–00:31]. This structural difference is the primary driver of performance divergence.
- **Year-to-date performance gap (as of video date):** IBIT down ~1.4%, BITO down ~12% [00:31–00:55]. The gap reflects the cost of rolling and maintaining a futures position versus holding spot.
- **Expense ratios and AUM favor IBIT** [00:31]. IBIT has lower fees and substantially higher assets under management.
- **Futures basis and roll costs disadvantage BITO in this market regime** [00:55]. When spot-futures spreads widen, the futures-based product underperforms.
- **Host uses IBIT as the underlying for covered strangle strategies** [00:55]. This is the vehicle for the strategy updates provided to viewers.
