# What Would You Tell a Close Friend? #shorts

## Summary

Eric poses a trading prompt to viewers following Outlier Trading Boot Camp Episode 2, which focused on risk management. The core question: what single piece of risk-management advice would you give a friend or relative entering investing? Eric's answer emphasizes favorable risk-to-reward ratios and preventing account-destroying losses.

## Key takeaways

- **Focus on risk-to-reward ratios** [00:15] — structure trades so potential gains justify potential losses; this is foundational to sustainable trading.
- **Prevent loss compounding** [00:25] — catastrophic losses that wipe out account capital make recovery mathematically impossible; capital preservation is prerequisite to profitability.
- **You can't trade if you don't exist** [00:28] — survival of the account is the first rule; blowups end the game.
