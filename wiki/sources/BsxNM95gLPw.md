# DO NOT HOVER OVER YOUR TRADES

## Summary
Eric discusses a live trade in DraftKings and emphasizes the importance of planning before entry and resisting the urge to constantly monitor or adjust positions. The core lesson: inaction and discipline often outperform reactive trading.

## Key takeaways
- **Plan before entry** [00:00] — Define your thesis and exit criteria before you pull the trigger; this prevents emotional decision-making mid-trade.
- **Do not hover over your trades** [00:00] — Constant monitoring and micro-adjustments typically degrade outcomes; trust your plan and let it work.
- **Sometimes doing nothing is the best course of action** [00:00] — Restraint and patience are underrated edges in trading.
