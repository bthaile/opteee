# Market Update: Quantum Computing, PPI, Stock Splits & Tesla

## Summary

A broad market update covering emerging quantum computing opportunities (QBTS, RGTI, IOQ, UBTS), inflation data (PPI came in hot at 0.4% MoM vs. 0.2% expected), sector rotation dynamics, and technical analysis of PANW (pre-split) and TSLA (recovery move into new territory). The host emphasizes building watchlists in early-stage quantum plays before widespread adoption, notes the importance of post-earnings drift for small/mid-cap stocks, and cautions that TSLA's recent rally—while impressive—remains primarily a recovery to 2021 peaks rather than new all-time highs.

## Key takeaways

- **Quantum computing as next wave**: Google's Willow project validates the technology; commercialization likely 10+ years away, but adoption timelines can accelerate rapidly with sufficient incentive. Small/mid-cap suppliers will move before large-scale deployment. [00:59–03:11]
- **Build watchlist now**: Companies like QBTS, RGTI, IOQ, UBTS are early-stage quantum plays with negative earnings but solid forecasts; most are still in red, typical for growth-stage tech. [07:22–08:26]
- **Post-earnings drift is real**: TWLO smashed earnings (beat by ~$0.92 on $0.08 consensus) and has floated higher; this is a key driver for small/mid-cap momentum. [07:22]
- **Sector rotation underway**: Discretionary crushing it; healthcare, utilities, real estate, energy, and industrials rolling over. Broad indices (SPX, QQQ) flat; small caps (IWM) rolling over. [08:26–09:45]
- **PPI inflation holding pattern**: PPI 0.4% MoM (vs. 0.2% expected, 0.3% prior); core PPI 0.2% in line. Inflation not tamped down; holding pattern is critical to market complacency. [09:45–10:52]
- **PANW pre-split setup**: Stock has had solid run; post-split often attracts new retail money. Nancy Pelosi's portfolio shows 800%+ returns on options strategies vs. 200% S&P 500 over same period. [10:52–11:58]
- **TSLA: recovery, not new highs**: Five-year chart shows TSLA just now returning to 2021 peaks; recent rally is impressive but still ~95% recovery move. New all-time territory only just beginning. [13:21]
- **Momentum entry risk**: TSLA post-extension entry is high-risk for swing/momentum trades; prefer to follow momentum but recognize late-stage entry hazard. [13:21]
