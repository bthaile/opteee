# 2023 Trading Year is CLOSED

## Summary

A year-end market recap and personal trading assessment. The S&P 500 delivered ~24% returns with a 10-year CAGR near 7%, establishing a high bar for active traders. The core message: underperformance relative to the market in a strong year signals a need for honest self-evaluation about trading approach and skill development.

## Key takeaways

- **Market performance baseline [00:00]**: S&P 500 returned approximately 24% in 2023; 10-year CAGR now ~7%. This is the benchmark against which to measure active trading results.
- **Underperformance is a red flag [00:22]**: Trailing the market for multiple years, especially in a year when the market "absolutely crushed it," demands a hard look at trading methodology and motivation.
- **Capital efficiency is the goal [00:22]**: If the objective is to grow capital as efficiently as possible, failing to pace the market in strong years is "a giant problem" unless actively closing the skill gap.
- **Trading is incremental progress [00:43]**: Every year matters; you cannot afford to waste a year. Approach 2024 tactically and intentionally.

## Notable quotes

- "Trading is a game of incremental progress you cannot waste any year you literally cannot afford it."
