# INSANE Email Regimen with Tom Sosnoff  #optionstrading

## Summary

Eric interviews Tom Sosnoff about his prolific email practice, which he has maintained for 24 years. Sosnoff describes sending hundreds of emails daily as a disciplined professional habit, carefully curated to engage only with customers and industry professionals while avoiding extraneous networking events.

## Key takeaways

- **Email volume and discipline** [00:37]: Sosnoff sends "hundreds easily hundreds" of emails daily, a practice he has sustained for 24 years as a core professional discipline.
- **Selective engagement** [00:55]: He is deliberate about only emailing back-and-forth with customers and professionals in the industry, actively avoiding professional events and non-essential networking.
- **Psychological framing** [00:17]: Sosnoff characterizes his email regimen as "a sickness," suggesting it is both compulsive and deeply ingrained in his work identity.

## Notable quotes
