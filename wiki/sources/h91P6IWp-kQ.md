# Options Trading Is All About Optimizing Processes #optionstrading

## Summary

Options trading success hinges on process optimization and iterative improvement rather than individual trades. By systematizing your workflow, reducing friction at each step, and conducting rigorous performance audits (after-action reviews), you accelerate learning cycles and uncover better trading ideas.

## Key takeaways

- **Process efficiency compounds**: the faster you iterate through your trading process, the more hypotheses you can test and the better ideas you'll discover [00:00–00:25]
- **Audit rigorously**: implement weekly, monthly, and annual after-action reviews (AARs) to verify that your trading system is operating as expected [00:25–00:52]
- **Optimize for speed and effectiveness**: focus on both how quickly you execute your process *and* how accurately you analyze your own performance [00:00–00:25]

## Notable quotes

> "This is a game of optimization. How well can you go through a process, make it better so that you can go through the process faster?"
