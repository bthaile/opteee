# How To Build A Trading Strategy with the Outlier Strategy Process #optionstrading

## Summary

Eric introduces the Outlier Strategy Process, a nested four-step framework for building trading strategies within a broader design methodology. The process emphasizes iterative testing and the reality that most strategy ideas fail, but those that work tend to be asymmetric—requiring patience and systematic exploration to discover.

## Key takeaways

- **The Outlier Strategy Process is a nested framework** [00:00] — a four-step methodology for strategy development embedded within a larger design process.
- **Most strategies don't work; asymmetry is the payoff** [00:28] — the gold-digging analogy: most exploration yields nothing, but successful strategies are typically highly asymmetric.
- **Strategy development is time-consuming but learnable** [00:51] — once you understand the systematic approach, execution becomes manageable.
- **Iterative testing is core** [00:00] — the framework relies on testing many ideas, accepting that a small percentage will prove viable.

## Notable quotes
