# Market Update: NVDA Earnings & GME Options Positioning

## Summary

Quick market update covering two earnings plays: NVDA reporting Wednesday (28th) with elevated implied volatility (77% raw, 97th percentile) and a history of upside bias in earnings moves; and GME with earnings on the 4th, showing unusual call skew (500K calls vs 264K puts) and a puzzling IV anomaly on September 13th that warrants investigation. Both positions are being managed with directional bias while hedging sideways oscillation.

## Key takeaways

### Dated market read (2024-08-28)

- **NVDA earnings setup** [00:00–07:14]: Earnings Wednesday after market; IV at 77% raw / 97th percentile; volume static on 5-day and 20-day averages; open interest roughly balanced (13.1M calls vs 13.1M puts) but call skew visible in front expiration (950K calls vs 788K puts). Historical earnings analysis shows upside bias (largest move +27%, downside capped around −5 to −7%), but valuation requires near-perfect delivery (EPS beat, revenue growth) to sustain.

- **NVDA valuation thesis** [06:00–07:14]: Stock has been valued into euphoria on AI narrative; earnings will test whether market is in mature phase or if parabolic upside can continue. Market is a discounting mechanism looking 6–9 months forward, not backward.

- **GME chart deterioration** [07:14–09:31]: 4-hour candles show consolidation breaking to downside over past two weeks; moving averages rotating over; RSI dropping below 4-period smoothing (momentum indicator of weakness); volume highly compressed (4.7M vs 5-day avg 3.7M, but still ~1M below 20-day avg). Earnings on the 4th before open.

- **GME options positioning** [11:40–13:42]: Long 5,000 shares with ratio call sales (e.g., 10–15 calls against 5,000 shares) to cap upside but profit on sideways oscillation while maintaining bullish exposure. Front expiration shows 500K calls vs 264K puts (nearly 2:1 ratio, bullish signal). **Anomaly**: earnings week IV at 72%, week after at 92.6%, then 87%—suggests market pricing something for September 13th; cause unknown.

- **GME time-and-sales** [13:42–17:10]: Blocks of 500 calls at 21.5 and 30 Aug; meaningful size building in 6-step and 13-step expirations (unusual whales tracking ~2K aggregate). Likely earnings directional plays; IV expansion expected even if underlying doesn't move much.

### Evergreen mechanics

- **IV percentile vs. IV rank**: Prefer IV percentile for cleaner signal; both measure relative volatility but percentile better isolates regime.
- **Earnings skew interpretation**: Puts skewed higher in mega-cap stocks (hedging fear of downside) is normal; not anomalous unless extreme.
- **Ratio call strategy**: Selling calls at ratio < 1:1 (e.g., 1 call per 100 shares) caps upside but generates premium while holding bullish directional bias; uncovered shares participate in large rallies.
- **RSI momentum tracking**: Slope of RSI and EMA over prior day; RSI dropping below smoothing period favors downside; flattening RSI signals add opportunity.
- **Volume profile in consolidation**: Compressed volume + moving average rotation = potential breakout; direction determined by price action (GME broke down).
