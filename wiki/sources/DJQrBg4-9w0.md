# Using a Trade Log

## Summary

This video addresses how to extract actionable insights from trade logs by analyzing the data systematically. Eric emphasizes starting with extreme outliers—both winners and losers—to identify patterns, then progressively narrowing focus to understand what strategies are performing well and why.

## Key takeaways

- **Start with extremes** [00:39]: When reviewing a trade log, first identify outlier trades (best and worst performers) across individual strategies and cross-strategy comparisons. This reveals which approaches are grossly outperforming or underperforming.
- **Systematic data review** [00:22]: Trade logs should tell a story about your edge. The question "what story are your data telling you?" is the core framework for log analysis.
- **Iterative narrowing** [00:39]: Begin at the tails of the distribution, then progressively zoom in to understand the mechanics of your wins and losses.
- **Community-driven content** [00:00]: Eric solicits questions from the Outlier Discord to identify real trader pain points rather than optimizing for algorithm performance.
