# Tech Falling and Trade Opportunities | Live Stock Market Analysis

## Summary

Market showing weakness concentrated in large-cap tech names (Nasdaq down ~2%, S&P down ~0.5%) despite muted VIX at 18.6, suggesting artificial volatility suppression. AMD and Palantir both cratered post-earnings despite beating estimates, indicating richly-priced tech facing overhead constraints and requiring near-perfect execution. Broader market rotation into energy, staples, materials, and industrials presents tactical opportunities; smaller-cap weakness offers higher-velocity downside trades for specialized traders.
