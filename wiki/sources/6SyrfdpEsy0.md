# Trading Plan for All Markets

## Summary

Eric outlines a systematic framework for constructing a diversified options toolkit that adapts to all market regimes. By mastering core option structures—directional singles, ratio diagonals, and volatility plays—and learning to apply them across bullish, bearish, and range-bound conditions, a trader can eliminate the problem of having "nothing to trade" regardless of market environment.

## Key takeaways

- **Bullish toolkit**: long single options, ratio diagonals, and directional overlays form the core bullish arsenal [00:00–00:28]
- **Bearish toolkit**: short single options, ratio diagonals, and directional structures for downside scenarios [00:00–00:28]
- **Volatility plays**: long straddles, short straddles, short strangles, and single options with directional overlays for elevated or compressed IV environments [00:00–00:28]
- **Core principle**: master a small set of basic option structures and learn to apply them across all market conditions rather than chasing exotic strategies [00:00–00:28]
- **Regime-agnostic trading**: by building capacity across directional and volatility dimensions, eliminate the trader's dilemma of having no viable setups in any given market state [00:00–00:28]
