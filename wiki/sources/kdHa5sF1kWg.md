# Trading EASY Button #trading #stockmarket #optionstrading

## Summary

This video identifies a single high-leverage practice that improves trading outcomes: defining profit and loss targets **before** entering every trade. While seemingly obvious, most traders skip this step because it requires confronting unknowable future outcomes. The discipline of pre-trade planning creates a decision-making framework that remains effective even when positions move against you.

## Key takeaways

- **Define P&L targets before entry [00:00]** — establish profit and loss targets as a non-negotiable pre-trade ritual; this forces clarity on risk/reward before emotion enters the picture.
- **Process discipline compounds [00:24]** — decision-making does not become easier once you're in a losing trade; the framework you build beforehand is your only reliable guide.
- **Confront uncertainty upfront [00:00]** — the event hasn't unfolded yet, so you're answering an unknowable question; but working through that process anyway trains better judgment as conditions evolve.

## Notable quotes

> "Define your profit and lost targets. I know nothing revolutionary, something that you should be doing already but most people don't do that."
