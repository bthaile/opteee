# An Unpopular Opinion on Taking Losses in Trading #shorts

## Summary

Taking losses is an essential and non-negotiable component of long-term trading success. Losses must be accepted as part of a disciplined risk-management plan, and while they should always sting emotionally, they are a necessary cost of staying in the game.

## Key takeaways

- **Losses are inevitable in trading** [00:00] — accepting them as part of your risk-management deployment is critical for longevity.
- **Emotional sting is healthy** [00:05] — losses should never feel easy; discomfort signals proper risk calibration and prevents recklessness.
- **Process over outcome** [00:10] — focus on the thought process behind each trade rather than dwelling on individual losing results.

## Notable quotes

> "Taking losses is part of trading if you want to be in the game long term we have to accept that taking losses is part of our risk management plan."
