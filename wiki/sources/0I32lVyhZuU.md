# Made $250,000+ TAX FREE Selling a Rental Property #shorts

## Summary

Eric discusses a ~$250k tax-free gain from selling a rental property held for over two years, leveraging the Section 1031 exchange or primary-residence exclusion. - **Capital rotation:** Realizing gains in one medium allows redeployment to higher-opportunity areas, compounding wealth-building velocity [00:10–00:20].
