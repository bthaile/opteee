# How the GameStop eBay Deal Can Close

## Summary

Eric outlines three potential paths for GameStop's approach to acquiring eBay: a negotiated deal with board cooperation, a hostile takeover via direct shareholder engagement and board replacement, or a failed bid scenario. The analysis focuses on the mechanics of how Cohen might execute each route, with emphasis on the hostile path's use of media announcement to bypass traditional board channels.

## Key takeaways

- **Negotiated path** [00:00]: eBay board takes the proposal to shareholders for an amicable deal structure.
- **Hostile path** [00:29]: Cohen approaches eBay shareholders directly, bypassing the board; secures controlling vote; installs sympathetic directors; proceeds to exchange tender offer.
- **Media announcement strategy** [00:29]: Cohen announced the bid publicly without addressing the board first, signaling intent to go directly to shareholders.
- **Failed bid scenario** [00:54]: The proposal is discussed but does not materialize; outcome remains uncertain at this early stage.
