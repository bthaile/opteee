# 0DTE Options RISK

## Summary

Zero-DTE (same-day expiration) options eliminate overnight risk but introduce concentrated intraday volatility exposure. While options are priced efficiently, a single adverse move can erase weeks or months of accumulated profits; conversely, buying 0DTE for small gains often fails to justify commissions and slippage. The strategy works best as one tool in a diversified toolkit rather than a standalone approach.

## Key takeaways

- **No overnight risk advantage is real but limited** [00:00] — 0DTE options expire the same day, eliminating gap risk, but this benefit is already priced into the option.
- **Efficient pricing means the edge is thin** [00:00–00:24] — selling 0DTE expecting worthless expiration requires only one large intraday move to wipe out weeks of profits.
- **Buying 0DTE for small gains rarely pencils out** [00:24–00:41] — time decay works against you; after commissions, fees, and slippage, the math often doesn't work.
- **Use 0DTE as part of a diversified toolkit, not your only strategy** [00:41] — apply it when it makes sense contextually, not as a default approach.
