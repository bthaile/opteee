# Why Trading Indicators Fail - Do This Instead

## Summary

The core principle for effective trading is understanding the **profit mechanism** first, then selecting indicators that align with that mechanism—not the reverse. This video demonstrates how sector rotation strategies leverage momentum as a profit mechanism, where outperforming sectors tend to persist until a trend reversal occurs.

## Key takeaways

- **Start with profit mechanism, not indicators** [00:00] — The relationship between the profit mechanism you're extracting and the signals/indicators that help identify it is foundational; indicators chosen without this understanding will fail.
- **Sector rotation built on momentum** [00:31] — Sectors that are performing well tend to continue performing well, and underperforming sectors tend to continue underperforming until a reversal occurs; this is the profit mechanism driving the strategy.
- **Trend reversal is the inflection point** [00:56] — Sector rotation strategies depend on identifying when a trend reversal is about to happen; this is where signal selection becomes critical.
