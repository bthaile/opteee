# Covered Call Option Strategy #optionstrading

## Summary

A covered call is constructed by holding 100 shares of stock and selling one short call against that position. The strategy reaches maximum profit when the long deltas (from shares) and short deltas (from the call) balance and cancel each other out, creating a delta-neutral position.

## Key takeaways

- **Position structure**: 100 shares + 1 short call = covered call [00:00]
- **Delta mechanics**: Long deltas from shares offset short deltas from the call; max profit occurs at delta equilibrium [00:00]
- **Lot sizing**: Options trade in 100-share contracts, so minimum position is 100 shares per short call [00:00]
