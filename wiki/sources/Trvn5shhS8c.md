# GameStop: Making Money While the Stock Declines

## Summary

GameStop has declined ~24% from recent highs and ~9.5% from the entry point of the covered-strangle position discussed, yet the position remains profitable due to short-premium collection and call-skew dynamics. With earnings scheduled for 25 March and elevated implied volatility (105% on the 28 March expiration vs. 83.6% on the prior), the strategy focuses on rolling positions to collect additional premium while maintaining long delta exposure through 250 shares at a $28.21 basis.
