# Know Yourself Improve Yourself Create Positive Habits #optionstrading

## Summary

This video emphasizes the foundational trader skill of ruthless self-awareness: identifying behavioral patterns that hurt or help your trading, then installing guard rails to enforce good habits. Eric illustrates with a personal example of impatience-driven trade setup errors, solved via a mandatory pre-trade checklist that eventually became automatic.

## Key takeaways

- **Self-awareness is prerequisite to improvement** [00:00] — You must be completely honest about your behavioral patterns before you can adjust them or lean into them.
- **Identify the root cause of your mistakes** [00:26] — Eric's impatience stemmed from feeling behind; this led to skipping critical checks (e.g., missing earnings dates) before entry.
- **Use guard rails, not willpower** [00:42] — A forced trade checklist eliminated the gap; over time, the discipline became habitual and the checklist became unnecessary.
- **Process beats outcome** — Establishing a repeatable, checked process removes the friction between intention and execution.

## Notable quotes

> "A big part of what you need to do as a trader is be completely and ruthlessly honest with yourself so that then you can either adjust behaviors that you know hurt you or embrace behaviors that you know help you."
