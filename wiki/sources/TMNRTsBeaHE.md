# Thinkorswim Analyze Tab: A Complete Walkthrough for Traders

## Summary

A comprehensive walkthrough of thinkorswim's Analyze tab, covering the risk profile, probability analysis, economic data, think back, fundamentals, and earnings tools. The video demonstrates how to use these features to visualize position P&L across price ranges, calculate probability of touch, and extract valuation and earnings context for informed trading decisions.

## Key takeaways

- **Risk Profile visualization** [00:39–01:48]: Y-axis shows position P&L (positive above zero, negative below); X-axis shows underlying prices. Vertical lines can be set to standard deviations (±1, ±2, ±3) or custom price levels (e.g., stop prices). Zoom and drag to adjust scale.

- **Standard deviation slices and probabilities** [02:34–03:17]: Hovering over ±1σ and ±2σ lines displays probability percentages automatically. Use "Set Slices" to switch between standard deviations, percentile steps, or custom intervals. Add or remove slices via the plus button.

- **Multi-leg position analysis** [03:37–05:15]: Different colored lines represent the same position at different expiration dates. Click individual legs to isolate and see how each performs independently. Adjust the metric to view total P&L, daily P&L, or Greeks (delta, gamma, theta, vega).

- **Probability of Touch mode** [05:41–06:26]: Preferred over probability in-the-money because it reflects management-based decisions at different price levels. Adjustable range (1σ, 2σ, 3σ) and time steps. Hover to see price, P&L, and Greeks at each point.

- **Probability Cone (projection)** [08:11–10:27]: Projects future price distribution using current volatility. X-axis is time (days forward), Y-axis is price. Crosshair shows probability of touching a given price by a given date. Useful for calculating expected return and managing position risk.

- **Economic Data tab** [10:49–11:10]: Plots broad economic indicators from FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data). Adjustable ranges and formats for macro context.

- **Think Back feature** [11:10–11:27]: On-demand historical replay—select a stock, date, and view the options chain and chart as they appeared then.

- **Fundamentals and valuation** [11:27–12:34]: View analyst ratings, download reports, access standard valuation metrics (annual/quarterly), and drill into market-cap breakdowns by business segment (revenue, competitors, trends).

- **Earnings context** [12:57–13:41]: Compare implied vs. historical volatility, straddle pricing, and actual vs. estimated earnings across quarters. Center line marks announcement; view 5 days before/after. Zoom and compare across multiple quarters.

- **Volatility and pricing adjustments** [07:28–08:11]: Adjust interest rates, volatility pricing models (binomial, Black-Scholes), and individual-leg volatility via "More Parameters" to stress-test position behavior.
