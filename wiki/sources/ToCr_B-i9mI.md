# Roaring Kitty's OLD GME Options Trades

## Summary

Eric reviews Roaring Kitty's earliest GameStop options trades from 2019–2021, reconstructed from Reddit post history. The analysis reveals that Roaring Kitty began with a pure options portfolio (LEAPs and longer-dated calls at various strikes), scaled into positions methodically over months, and only introduced equity shares in April 2020—contradicting the common perception that he was primarily a stock trader using options for amplification. His account grew from ~$100k to $34.4M by April 2021, then to ~$250M by June 2021.

## Key takeaways

- **Pure options entry [01:42–02:03]**: Roaring Kitty's entire early portfolio was options-only; no stock positions visible until April 2020. This reframes him as an options trader first, not a stock trader using leverage.

- **Scaling into the same strike over time [02:29–04:21]**: He began with 50 contracts, then added 50, then 100+ at the same strike across multiple months (September–December 2019), buying at prices from $60 down to $30. This is deliberate scaling into a thesis, not panic averaging.

- **LEAPs as the core vehicle [04:47–05:11]**: His first major position (September 2019) was in 2021 LEAPs—far out-of-the-money calls. This long-dated approach suggests he was trading a multi-year thesis, not short-term catalysts.

- **Delta-based strike selection [10:14–10:41]**: Across expirations, he favored out-of-the-money strikes (8, 10, 15, 20 calls when stock was ~$4) with consistent delta preference, not random strikes.

- **Transition to equity [08:33–09:27]**: First shares appear in February 2020 (~$1,400 notional); by May 2020, he was building equity alongside options. By July 2020, equity became the dominant position.

- **Account volatility and conviction [12:15–12:40]**: His account swung from $22M to $48M to $33M to $26M without panic selling—demonstrating extreme risk tolerance and conviction in the thesis.

- **Final update timing [13:01–13:16]**: Last public update was April 16, 2021 ($34.4M); next visible update June 2021 ($250M). Eric speculates the April timing may relate to Congressional testimony.
