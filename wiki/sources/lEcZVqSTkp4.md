# PLAN

## Summary

A foundational lesson on the necessity of deliberate planning in trading. The video emphasizes that without a structured plan, retail traders face systematic disadvantage against well-resourced market participants who invest time in research and strategy execution.

## Key takeaways

- **Planning is non-negotiable** [00:00] — traders who skip planning will be "eaten alive" by professionals who dedicate resources to research and strategy.
- **Asymmetric preparation** [00:00] — institutional and serious retail participants invest heavily in planning and research specifically to exploit unprepared traders.
- **Competitive disadvantage without process** [00:00] — the stock market contains many actors explicitly seeking to extract capital from underprepared participants.

## Notable quotes

> "If you do not take the time to plan you are going to get eaten alive."
