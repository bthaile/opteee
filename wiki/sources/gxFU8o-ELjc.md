# Delayed Gratification #investing #stockmarket

## Summary

A brief educational segment on the foundational principle of delayed gratification in investing and personal finance. The core message: embracing delayed gratification early—particularly in your 20s—compounds into significant financial advantage by your 30s and beyond. Starting the compounding clock early is the single most impactful lever for long-term wealth building.

## Key takeaways

- **Delayed gratification is a prerequisite for wealth.** Most things worth having in life require it; in finance, this principle is non-negotiable [00:00].
- **Time is the most powerful variable.** The sooner you start investing, the more compounding works in your favor; those who embrace this in their 20s see outsized results by their 30s [00:00].
- **Process over outcome.** Learning and embracing the *concept* of delayed gratification is the gateway to consistent financial behavior [00:00].
