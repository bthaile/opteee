# Institutions VS Your WSB Plays

## Summary

This video contrasts the operational scale and infrastructure of institutional market makers (using Susquehanna International Group as a case study) against retail traders on Wall Street Bets. While institutions maintain global hiring footprints, extensive trade support, and sophisticated operations, the video acknowledges that retail traders can still achieve profitability despite the asymmetry.

## Key takeaways

- **Institutional scale**: SIG maintains ~50 open trader positions globally (Hong Kong, Ireland, UK, Saudi Arabia, Sydney, Australia) plus extensive trade support infrastructure [00:00–00:26]
- **Retail accessibility**: Despite the institutional advantage, it is genuinely possible for retail traders to perform well [00:52]
- **Infrastructure gap**: Institutions operate with supporting systems (trade support analysts, compliance, risk management) that retail traders typically lack [00:26–00:52]
