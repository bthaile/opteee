# The Gordon Ramsay Approach to Options Trading #optionstrading

## Summary

This video reframes options trading through the lens of process mastery rather than individual trade outcomes. Drawing an analogy to Gordon Ramsay's culinary empire, the core thesis is that successful trading requires building a repeatable, learnable process that generates consistent edge over thousands of trades—not obsessing over any single position's result.

## Key takeaways

- **Process over outcome** [00:00–00:30]: Shift focus from individual trade results to the broader strategy executed repeatedly. Each trade is a sample within a larger statistical framework.
- **Sample size matters** [00:30]: Long-term traders are measured across tens of thousands of trades, not individual wins or losses. This aligns with [[law-of-large-numbers]] and [[process-over-outcome]].
- **Replicable system building**: Like Ramsay's culinary process, trading success comes from designing a system that others can learn and execute, not from isolated genius trades.
