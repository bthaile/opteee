# UPRO

UPRO is a 3x leveraged ETF tracking the S&P 500 that serves as an underlying for covered strangle positions in options trading. Traders use UPRO alongside other leveraged and non-leveraged ETFs (such as TQQQ and SPY) to construct diversified portfolios that test different volatility regimes and leverage profiles while managing capital allocation and delta-neutral positioning. In portfolio rebalancing contexts, UPRO positions are adjusted through rolling short puts and calls to optimize risk-adjusted returns and reduce cash-secured requirements while maintaining defensive market exposure.
